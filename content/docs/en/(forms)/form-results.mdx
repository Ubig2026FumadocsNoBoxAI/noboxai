Form Results is a mission-critical module in NoBox.AI designed for the systematic management and processing of all incoming form submissions.

This environment enables team members to monitor incoming orders, adjust product lists, override costs, initiate automated customer follow-ups, and update both payment and sales delivery statuses within a unified interface.

## **Product Management & Order Processing**

1. Navigate to the **[Form Results]** menu on your NoBox.AI dashboard.
2. Select a specific submission (typically an 'Order Form' type) that you wish to process or modify.
3. The system will display the comprehensive detail view, including a line-item list of all ordered products.

### **Inventory Adjustments within Results**

The individual result page allows you to make real-time corrections to a customer's order:

- **Add Products**: Click the add product button to include additional items requested by the customer after their initial submission.
- **Remove Products**: Identify items that are out of stock or cancelled, and remove them from the order.
- **Update Quantities**: Adjust the number of items per product line based on customer interaction.
- Click **[Save]** to ensure the new order total and inventory data are updated across the system.

## **Cost Overrides & Fee Management**

To add shipping fees or apply manual discounts to a specific result, follow these steps:

1. Select the form result record you wish to modify.
2. Scroll to the **Cost Summary** section at the bottom of the page.
3. Use the **Additions** field for extra costs (e.g., shipping, gift wrapping) or the **Deductions** field for manual discounts.
4. The system will automatically recalculate the grand total in real-time.
5. Click **[Save]** to finalize the updated transaction value.

## **Customer Follow-Up Procedures**

The Form Results module facilitates rapid communication with respondents to confirm details or provide transactional updates.

1. Open the specific form result you wish to follow up on.
2. Click the **[Follow Up]** icon.
3. Choose a pre-defined follow-up category. NoBox.AI will automatically populate the message template preview.
4. Modify the message content if specific personalization is required.
5. Click **[Send]** (dispatch occurs via WhatsApp or Email integration).

## **Status Lifecycle Management**

### **Updating Payment Statuses**

Keeping payment information accurate is essential for revenue tracking:

1. Locate the specific result in the dashboard or detail view.
2. Toggle the payment status to match the actual condition, such as "Paid," "Unpaid," or "Deposit Received."
3. Click **[Save]** to synchronize this data with the Finance and Reporting modules.

### **Updating Sales & Delivery Statuses**

Track the physical or digital journey of the order for the customer:

1. Select the target form result.
2. Update the Sales Status to reflect the current phase, such as "Packing," "Shipped," or "Delivered."
3. Click **[Save]** to ensure the entire team is aware of the current fulfillment stage.

## **Workflow Optimization Tips**

To master the Form Results management process:

- **Verification First**: Always double-check customer product requests and manual price overrides before clicking Save; this avoids billing disputes.
- **Instant Status Sync**: Update payment statuses as soon as bank transfer confirmations are received to keep the administrative queue clean.
- **Consistent Communication**: Use the automated Follow-Up feature immediately after a submission to acknowledge receipt and build customer trust.
- **Reporting Integrity**: Consistently use the Sales Status markers so that management can generate accurate "Fulfillment Speed" reports.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience difficulties while managing form results:

- Refresh the system page if newly submitted results are not appearing in the list.
- Verify internet stability during large batch status updates.
- Ensure your account permissions are correctly set for editing sales and payment data.
- Log out and log back in to refresh your session if the "Follow-Up" button becomes unresponsive.
- Contact NoBox support if automated messages are stuck or not being delivered.
