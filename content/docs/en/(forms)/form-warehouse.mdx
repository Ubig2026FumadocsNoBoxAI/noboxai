**Form Warehouse** is a dedicated module in NoBox.AI used to manage storage locations and inventory origins within the system.

This menu is critical for effective inventory management and logistics, as it defines the physical points from which products are shipped and helps organize distribution channels for all orders processed through your digital forms.

### **Warehouse Form Fields**

Complete the required fields accurately to ensure smooth logistics operations:

| Field    | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   | Example       |
| -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| Name     | The primary identity of the storage facility. This name will appear in warehouse lists, transactions, and reports. Use unique and descriptive names based on location or function.                                                           | Milan Hub     |
| Phone    | Contact number for the warehouse staff. This is used for logistics coordination, shipping confirmations, and on-site troubleshooting. Ensure the number is active and valid.                                                                  | 0813102007221 |
| Country  | The nation where the warehouse is located. This supports global region settings and cross-border shipping requirements.                                                                                                                      | Indonesia     |
| State    | The province or administrative region. Helps in grouping distribution areas and regional market analysis.                                                                                                                                     | East Java     |
| City     | The specific city or district. This data is essential for calculating delivery routes, estimating arrival times, and managing local logistics.                                                                                               | Malang City   |
| Zip Code | The postal code for the facility. Used for address validation and sorting by couriers to minimize delivery errors.                                                                                                                            | 65124         |
| Address  | Full street address, including building numbers and additional landmarks. This is the primary reference for all physical field operations and courier pickups.                                                                                | Akordion St.  |

Once the information is verified, click **[Save]** to finalize the new warehouse record.

## **Managing Warehouse Data**

### Editing Warehouse Records

To update a warehouse profile, select the target record from your list. Modify the necessary details—such as updating a phone number or adding more address specificities—and click **[Save]**. 

Updates are synchronized across all connected modules immediately.

### Deleting Warehouse Records

To remove a facility from the system, select the target record and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation prompt will appear to prevent accidental removal.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Warehouse deletion is permanent. Ensure that no active orders or current stock levels are linked to the specific warehouse before proceeding with removal.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Operational Tips**

- **Descriptive Naming**: Use names that are easy to distinguish (e.g., "North Jakarta Hub" vs "West Jakarta Hub") to avoid confusion during fulfillment.
- **Accurate Coordinates**: Ensure Zip Codes and full addresses are correct; this directly impacts the accuracy of automated shipping rate calculations.
- **Contact Availability**: Always provide a phone number that is monitored during business hours to assist couriers with pickups.
- **Regular Audits**: Update your warehouse list if you move locations or close a facility to keep your logistics data clean.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience issues while using the Form Warehouse module:

- Refresh the page if the warehouse list fails to refresh after a save.
- Verify that your internet connection is stable for real-time data entry.
- Ensure your account has "Administrator" or "Manager" level permissions.
- Log out and log back in if the "Save" button becomes unresponsive.
- Ensure all mandatory fields marked with an asterisk are filled using the correct data format.
