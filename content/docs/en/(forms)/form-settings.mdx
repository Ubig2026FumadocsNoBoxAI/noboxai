Form Settings is the foundational configuration module in NoBox.AI that allows you to define the default formatting and behavior for all your form models.

By establishing global preferences in this menu, users can ensure consistency across multiple digital forms and significantly reduce manual setup time when initializing new form templates.

## **Categorized Configuration Options**

The settings menu is divided into several detailed sections:

### 1. **General Presets**

This section is used to **set the default form type**.  
Configuring this ensures that whenever a new form template is created, the system automatically selects your preferred type (e.g., Order Form), maintaining consistency and speeding up deployment.

### 2. **Default Payment Methods**

Establish which **payment options appear by default** on every new form.  
This prevents the need for repetitive manual configuration for each individual product form, ensuring your preferred payment gateways are always available to customers.

### 3. **Post-Submission Behavior**

Defines **what the customer sees after clicking 'Submit'**.  
Options include redirecting the user to a custom "Success" landing page, displaying an on-screen thank-you message, or triggering specific URL-based actions.

### 4. **Automated Follow-Ups**

Configure the **default auto-response templates** sent after a form is completed.  
These templates can include personalized text, branded emails, or specific transactional instructions to ensure immediate and professional communication with every respondent.

### 5. **Form Utilities & Options**

Provides additional functional toggles for your form environment:

- **Notification Dispatch (WA, Email, URL)**:  
  Set the default platforms for sending automated notifications and follow-up alerts to respondents.

- **Visual Aids & Security (Labels, Recaptcha, Coupons)**:  
  Toggle the default visibility of input labels, activate Google Recaptcha for bot protection, and enable coupon input fields globally.

### 6. **System Audit Trail**

Displays a record of when the **settings were last updated**, including the timestamp and the specific user responsible for the changes. This is invaluable for internal monitoring and administrative accountability.

---

## **Applying and Verifying Changes**

After adjusting your global preferences, click **[Save]** to apply the new logic.

To verify, navigate to **[Form Templates]** ➔ **[Add Form Template]**. You will see that the new creation dialog already contains the defaults you established in the settings module.

---

## **Resetting to System Defaults**

The **Reset** feature (Atur Ulang) allows you to revert every form preference back to its original factory configuration.  
Use this tool if you want to clear complex configurations or begin a fresh setup of your forms module from scratch.

<Tips>
## **Optimization Tips**

- **Business Alignment**: Map your default "After Submission" actions to your actual marketing funnel for maximum efficiency.
- **Verification Cycle**: Always double-check your default payment methods to ensure they match your current billing accounts.
- **Manual Overrides**: Remember that global settings are _defaults_; you can still override these specific choices for individual forms if a unique campaign requires it.
- **Monitoring**: Check the audit trail regularly if you work in a multi-administrator environment to stay informed of logic changes.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience issues within the Form Settings module:

- Refresh the system page if newly saved settings fail to persist.
- Verify your internet connectivity for reliable server synchronization.
- Ensure your user role has the "Administrator" permissions required to modify global logic.
- Log out and log back in if the "Save" or "Reset" functions appear unresponsive.
- Ensure all automated message templates are formatted correctly to prevent dispatch errors.

</Kendala>
