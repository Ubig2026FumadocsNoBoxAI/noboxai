Payment Methods is a master data module in NoBox.AI used to configure and manage the different ways your customers can pay for orders.

This menu streamlines the setup of various gateways, including bank transfers, digital e-wallets, and manual cash-on-delivery options, ensuring that accurate payment instructions are always attached to your digital forms and transactions.

## **Adding a New Payment Method**

1. Navigate to the **[Payment Methods]** menu ➔ **[Add Payment Method]**.
2. An **[Add Payment Method]** dialog will appear. Complete the Title, Type, and specific payment instructions.

### **Form Configuration Fields**

| **Field**    | **Description**                       | **Example**                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Type         | General payment category              | Bank Transfer                                                                                                                                             |
| Title/Name   | Public-facing name of the method      | Mandiri Bank                                                                                                                                              |
| Status       | Toggle the availability of the method | Active                                                                                                                                                    |
| Notes        | Internal administrative context       | -                                                                                                                                                         |
| Instructions | Granular payment guide for customers  | Please transfer to Mandiri Bank A/C xxxx.xxxx.xxxx (A/N Jasmine Aulya). After the transfer, please send proof of payment to our WhatsApp at 62xxxxxxxxxx. |
| QR Code      | Visual payment trigger (e.g., QRIS)   | -                                                                                                                                                         |

Once all fields are complete, click **[Save]** to list the new method.

## **Managing Payment Records**

### Editing Payment Methods

To modify an existing payment option, select the record from the dashboard. Update individual fields—such as changing bank account numbers or updating the QR code—and click **[Save]**.

Changes are immediately available for any new form submissions or transactions.

### Deleting Payment Methods

To remove a method, select the entry and click **[Delete]**. Verify the action in the secondary confirmation prompt.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
  Deletion of a payment method is permanent. Ensure it is not being used as the
  primary payment option in any "Active" digital forms before proceeding with
  removal.
</Callout>

## **Applying Payment Methods to Forms**

Once your payment master data is saved, you can link these methods to specific marketing or order forms:

1. Open the target **Form Template** for editing.
2. Select the desired payment methods from the "Payment Method" multi-select list.
3. Click **[Save]** to apply the configuration.

<Tips>
## **Optimization Tips**

- **Verification**: Ensure the instructions provided match the active bank account or e-wallet to avoid delayed payments.
- **Clarity**: Use simple, step-by-step text in the **Instructions** field (e.g., "Step 1: Transfer, Step 2: Upload").
- **Visuals**: Always upload a high-quality QR Code to minimize data entry errors by the customer.
- **Audit**: Regularly check that the "Active" status correctly reflects the billing accounts currently available to your business.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience issues with the Payment Methods module:

- **Missing Options**: Ensure the method is set to **Status: Active**; inactive methods will not appear in form configuration lists.
- **Instruction Visibility**: If instructions are not appearing on the form, verify that the text has been successfully saved in the **Instructions** field.
- **QR Code Rendering**: If the QR Code is broken, re-upload the file in a standard web format (JPG, PNG).
- **Update Delay**: Refresh your browser or clear the cache if changes made to payment data are not showing up in the user-facing forms immediately.

</Kendala>
