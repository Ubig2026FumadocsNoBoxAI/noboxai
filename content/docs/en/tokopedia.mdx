Tokopedia Automation in NoBox.AI allows you to manage buyer interactions on the Tokopedia marketplace automatically and efficiently. With this feature, you can send mass follow-up messages to customers, quickly reply to buyer chats through a centralized dashboard, and ensure your shop performance remains optimal.

This tool is very helpful for online retailers in increasing customer satisfaction through rapid responses without having to manually drain your daily operational time.

## **Connecting a Tokopedia Account**

The first step to using this feature is registering your Tokopedia Seller account into the NoBox system.

### **Account Addition Steps:**

1. Click the **[Account]** module ➔ **[Add Account]** button.
2. Select the **Tokopedia** channel and complete your account information details.
3. Click **[Save]** to register the account into the system.

## **Configuration and Session Activation**

After the account is registered, you must activate the login session in NoBox Desktop so that messages can be synchronized in real-time.

### **How to Activate Session:**
- Open the **NoBox Desktop (Chat Session)** interface.
- The newly created Tokopedia account will appear in the list. Click and log in through the internal Tokopedia browser that appears.
- Once successfully logged in, click the **[Save Session]** button.
- Ensure a confirmation "Save session successfully" appears in the logs tab. Your account is now active in the NoBox.AI dashboard.

## **Messaging and Monitoring Management**

### **Reply via Inbox**
All messages entering your Tokopedia account will be automatically pulled into the **[Messages]** ➔ **[Inbox]** menu. You can sort messages, assign labels, and reply to customer chats directly from the dashboard.

## **Editing and Deleting Account Data**

### **Edit Account Data**
Navigate to the **[Account]** menu on the NoBox.AI website. Select the account you want to adjust info for, make changes, and then click **[Save]**.

### **Delete Account Data**
To delete an account from the system, select the relevant account and click the **[Delete]** button.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Account deletion is permanent and will stop all Tokopedia message synchronization processes to the NoBox.AI system. Ensure no active campaigns are running using that account before deleting it.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Some tips for maximizing Tokopedia Automation:

- Always monitor the **Logs** tab in NoBox Desktop to ensure the session status remains **Connected**.
- Use the **Quick Reply** feature to answer frequently asked buyer questions about stock or shipping.
- Periodically clear cache if message synchronization feels slow.
- Ensure you are not using the Tokopedia Seller application on another device that causes the previous login session to disconnect automatically (*multi-login issue*).

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues in the Tokopedia Automation service, please follow this guide:

- **Sessions Disconnect Frequently**: This usually happens if you log in to Tokopedia services on another device. Perform a re-login in NoBox Desktop and click **Save Session** again.
- **Message Data Not Entering**: Check your internet connection or ensure your computer firewall allows NoBox Desktop connection access.
- **Fail to Login (OTP)**: If Tokopedia asks for OTP verification, ensure you have access to the registered phone number to enter the code in the NoBox Desktop browser window.
- **Broadcast Messages Delayed**: Check if Tokopedia sending limits have been reached or reduce the number of mass deliveries per session.
- **Blank Page**: Refresh the page or clear your Google Chrome browser cache.

</Kendala>
