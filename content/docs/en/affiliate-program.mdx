The NoBox.AI Affiliate Program is a partnership scheme that allows you to earn additional income by promoting NoBox.AI products. You are entitled to a 30% commission from every product sale made through your affiliate link.

All sales data, customer history, and commission withdrawals are transparently managed through your Affiliate dashboard system.

## **Product Management (Products List)**

This page is used to manage the list of products you want to display on your Affiliate Store.

### Adding Products to Store:
1. Select the **[Products List]** menu ➔ Click the **[Add Products]** button.
2. Choose the available NoBox products to promote.
3. Click **[Save]** to update your store view.

To see the store as your customers see it, use the **[Preview Store]** feature.

### Remove Products from Store
If you want to stop promoting a certain product, select the product and click the delete icon (red cross/trash can).

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Removing a product from the affiliate store is permanent. The affiliate link for that product will no longer work on your store page.
</Callout>

## **Commission Withdrawal (Transactions)**

Once a customer's transaction is **Paid**, the commission will hit your account balance. You can request a withdrawal through the **Transactions** menu.

### How to Withdraw:
1. Check the commission data that has a **Paid** status.
2. Click the **[Withdraw all selected]** button.
3. Fill in the bank details and payment method in the dialog that appears.

### Commission and Withdrawal Status:

| Status | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Unpaid** | Commission is still pending (order not yet paid). |
| **Waiting** | Withdrawal request is being processed by the admin. |
| **Reject** | Withdrawal request was rejected due to data issues. |
| **Accept/Paid** | Withdrawal funds have been successfully sent to your account. |

## **Usage Tips**

Maximize your earnings with these tips:

- Use the **Preview Store** feature to ensure your product display is attractive to customers.
- Monitor the **My Logs** menu periodically to detect new orders from your customers.
- Always update your bank account details so the **Withdraw** process is not hindered.
- Share your affiliate link on social media or community forums to increase sales opportunities.
- Focus promotions on NoBox products that are currently most in demand by the market.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while running the affiliate program, follow these guidelines:

- **Commission Does Not Appear**: Ensure the customer has paid the invoice in full (**Paid**).
- **Withdrawal Failed**: Double-check if the bank account number entered is correct and matches the account owner's name.
- **Affiliate Link Not Working**: Check if the relevant product is still in your **Products List**.
- **Tenant ID Scan Failed**: Ensure the customer provides the correct Tenant ID from their company profile on NoBox.AI.
- **Report Data Not Updating**: Refresh the page or use the appropriate date period filter.

</Kendala>
