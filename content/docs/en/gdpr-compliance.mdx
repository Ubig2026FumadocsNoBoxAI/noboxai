GDPR (*General Data Protection Regulation*) is the strictest data protection standard in the world set by the European Union. NoBox.AI realizes that customer data is a very valuable asset regardless of where they are located. Therefore, we have adopted GDPR principles as our global data security base standard.

This page contains details of the technical and organizational efforts we undertake to fully comply with this regulation.

---

## **What is GDPR and Personal Data?**

This regulation aims to provide full control to individuals over their data. Protected personal data covers a broad spectrum, ranging from names, emails, IP addresses, to biometric and financial data. NoBox only processes information that is absolutely necessary for product operations, in accordance with the "Data Minimization" principle.

---

## **NoBox.AI Readiness Towards GDPR**

We have undertaken comprehensive steps across all organizational lines:

### 1. Privacy Conscious Culture
We provide ongoing training to all employees regarding information security standards and user data handling ethics in accordance with GDPR standards.

### 2. Information Asset Register (IAR)
We record all categories of processed personal data, who has access, and for what purpose. This ensures data flow in the NoBox system is always auditable.

### 3. Data Protection Officer (DPO)
We have appointed a *Data Protection Officer* (DPO) and privacy officers in each of our internal teams to oversee the compliance of each of our products.

### 4. Privacy by Design
Our development team implements the *Privacy by Design* concept in every new feature, giving users greater control to delete or manage their own data.

---

## **Security Methods and Your Rights**

- **Data Encryption**: We encrypt data both at rest and in transit using the latest security protocols.
- **Regular Internal Audits**: We perform audits on every operational process to ensure no privacy gaps are open.
- **Breach Notification**: We are committed to providing information within 72 hours if a serious incident related to your personal data occurs.
- **Right to be Forgotten**: You can request data deletion or a copy of personal data through our official channels.
