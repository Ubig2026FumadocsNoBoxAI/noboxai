**Human Agents** in NoBox.Ai is the control center for managing all human users who have access to your platform. This feature allows you to invite team members, set roles, and control their access rights according to your company's organizational structure.

While AI Agents perform tasks automatically, Human Agents are your operational team—such as customer service staff, managers, and administrators—who oversee and manage customer interactions directly.

## **Inviting New Agents**

Adding Human Agents is done through an invitation system to maintain company data security.

### **How to Invite an Agent:**

1. Click the **[Copy Invitation]** button at the top right of the page.
2. A unique invitation link will be automatically copied to your clipboard.
3. Send the link to potential team members through your favorite communication media (WA, Email, etc.).
4. After the agent registers via the link, their account will appear in the list with an active status.

The **[Unlimited]** label indicates that you have the freedom to add as many agents as needed according to your business scalability without additional costs per agent.

---

## **Editing and Deleting Human Agent Data**

### **Edit Agent Data**
To change agent information such as role assignment, select the target agent's name. Make changes to the agent profile form, then click **[Save]**.

### **Delete Agent Data**
Select the agent you want to remove from the system, click the **Delete (trash can)** icon, and confirm your choice in the dialog that appears.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting Human Agent data is permanent. Deleted agents will not be able to log in to the system, and all their access rights will be revoked immediately.
</Callout>

## **Usage Tips**

Some tips for managing Human Agents:

- Assign roles that match each team member's job description to maintain data security.
- Use official company email addresses for each agent to look more professional.
- Periodically monitor the status column to ensure all team members have completed email verification.
- Utilize the unlimited invitation feature to distribute workload to more agents when message traffic is high.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues in agent management, please follow this guide:

- **Invitation Link Not Working**: Try regenerating the invitation link by clicking the **[Copy Invitation]** button again.
- **Agent Not Appearing in List**: Ensure team members have completed the registration process through the provided link.
- **Verification Email Not Received**: Suggest agents check their Spam folder or click the resend verification button on their account.
- **Failed to Change Roles**: Ensure you are logged in with an administrator account (Owner/Manager) that has team management authorization.
- **Agent Forgot Password**: Direct the agent to the login page and click the "Forgot Password" link to perform a self-reset.

</Kendala>
