## **Main Modules of NoBox.AI**

NoBox.AI consists of several core modules that are integrated to support comprehensive business operations, from customer communication to system management.

1. **Messages**  
   The module used to manage all customer conversations from various communication channels. It helps customer service teams reply to chats, handle broadcast campaign messages, and monitor conversation history centrally.

2. **CRM**  
   Functions to store and manage customer data, including identity, communication history, lead status, and sales activities.

3. **Forms**  
   Used to create digital forms such as registrations, surveys, and customer data collection that are automatically saved in the system.

4. **Promotions**  
   Supports sending promotional messages, notifications, and scheduled marketing campaigns across multiple channels.

5. **Contacts**  
   Used to manage the customer database containing names, phone numbers, emails, categories, and other supporting information.

6. **Accounts**  
   Functions to set up user account data, licenses, service status, and tool management.

7. **AI Agents**  
   Used to manage AI-based chatbots that serve customers automatically according to defined scenarios.

8. **Human Agents**  
   Used to set up human agents who handle follow-up conversations if the AI cannot resolve a customer request.

9. **Subscription**  
   Contains information about service packages, subscription active periods, and available features.

10. **Billing**  
    Used to manage billing, invoices, payment history, and transaction status.

11. **Settings**  
    Used for overall system configuration, including:
    - **User Profile**: Personal data, date, time, and number formats.
    - **Business Profile**: Company information and operating hours.
    - **File Manager**: Management of files and documents.
    - **Custom Inputs**: Additional fields based on business needs.
    - **Contact Tools**: Management and integration of contacts.
