## **Adding a Product**

1. To list a new product, navigate to the **[Product]** menu ➔ **[Add Product]**.
2. An **[Add Product]** dialog will appear. Complete the required fields for the new item.

### Add Product Dialog View

This form is designed to capture every essential detail needed to present your product effectively within the sales pipeline.

### Add Product Form Fields

| Field              | Description                            | Example              |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Name               | Name of the Product                    | Decorative Lamp      |
| Product Code       | Unique SKU or identifier               | 00010                |
| Unit               | Measurement unit                       | Pcs                  |
| Currency & Price   | Select currency and set base price     | Rp 15,000            |
| Discount           | Applicable percentage discount         | 15                   |
| Sale Price         | Final price after discount (automated) | 12,750               |
| Manufacturer       | Product industry or manufacturer       | -                    |
| Tags               | Relevant product tags                  | Electronics          |
| Category           | Linked product category                | Holiday Supplies     |
| Description        | Detailed product summary               | Beautiful decorative lamp |
| Multi-order View   | Enable/Disable in multi-product orders | Enabled              |
| Image              | High-quality product image             | -                    |

Once you have completed the information, click **[Save]** to add the product to your catalog.

## **Editing and Deleting Product Records**

### Editing Products

To update an existing product, select the specific item from your dashboard. Modify the necessary fields and click **[Save]** to apply the updates.

Changes are synchronized immediately across all active sales deals and catalogs.

### Deleting Products

To remove a product entry, select the item and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation prompt will appear to verify the removal.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Product deletion is permanent. Ensure that the item is no longer needed in historical reports or active deals before proceeding.
</Callout>

## **Usage Tips**

To optimize your inventory management:

- **Unique SKU Usage**: Consistently use unique product codes for accurate stock tracking.
- **Detailed Profiles**: Complete every data field to improve searching and filtering.
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Regularly update your prices to stay competitive with market shifts.
- **Categorization**: Use accurate categories and tags to streamline the sales team's workflow.
- **Visual Appeal**: Upload clear, professional images to assist agents during customer interactions.

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while managing your products:

- Refresh your browser page to ensure current server synchronization.
- Verify your internet connectivity for stable data transmission.
- Confirm your account has the "Manager" or "Supervisor" permissions required for inventory edits.
- Try logging out and back in if the "Save" button becomes unresponsive.
- Contact the NoBox support team if you notice data discrepancies in your catalog.
