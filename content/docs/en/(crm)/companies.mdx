## **Adding a Company**

1. To create a new company profile, navigate to the **[CRM]** module ➔ click the **[Companies]** menu ➔ **[Companies]** ➔ **[Add Company]**.
2. An **[Add Company]** dialog will appear. Complete the required fields within the form.

## **Editing and Deleting Company Data**

### Editing Company Records

To modify an existing company profile, select the specific record from the list. Update the necessary information and click **[Save]** to apply the changes.

The updated data will be immediately synchronized across the system.

### Deleting Company Records

To remove a company entry, select the record and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation prompt will appear asking for verification.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting a company record is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure that the data is no longer required before proceeding with the deletion.
</Callout>

## **Editing and Deleting Tasks**

### Editing Tasks

Select the task you wish to modify, make the necessary updates, and click **[Save]**.

Changes will be reflected across both the CRM and the global Task management system.

### Deleting Tasks

Select the task and click **[Delete]**. Confirm the action when prompted.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Task deletion is permanent. Ensure that the action is intentional as the data cannot be recovered.
</Callout>

## **Editing and Deleting Notes**

Note management permissions are restricted to **NoBox.AI Supervisors**.

**Agents** can add new notes but do not have the authorization to edit or delete them.

### Editing Notes

Click the [ <img src="/images/id/Edit.png" alt="Edit" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] icon to modify the content of a note.

### Deleting Notes

Click the [ <img src="/images/id/Hapus.png" alt="Delete" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] icon to remove a note.

Confirm by selecting **[Yes]** or **[No]**.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Note deletion is permanent. Ensure the information is truly no longer needed before removal.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Maximize your efficiency in the Companies module with these best practices:

- Ensure all company data is detailed and accurate for better reporting.
- Use official company names to maintain record consistency.
- Regularly update contact information to avoid communication gaps.
- Leverage Notes to record unique client preferences or historical contexts.
- Use Tasks to schedule follow-ups and ensure no lead is forgotten.
- Monitor Activity Logs to understand the full history of the relationship.
- Attach relevant supporting documents to keep all materials in one place.
- Perform routine database audits to keep records organized.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while using the Companies menu, follow these steps:

- Verify that your internet connection is stable.
- Refresh the browser page if data fails to populate.
- Confirm that your account has the correct permissions for the module.
- Ensure all mandatory fields (marked with an asterisk) are completed when saving.
- Try logging out and back in if features seem unresponsive.
- Clear your browser cache if the interface display is abnormal.
- Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.
- Contact the support team if technical difficulties persist.
