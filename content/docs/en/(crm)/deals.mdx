The **Deals** module is a central feature of NoBox.AI designed for comprehensive sales pipeline management. It allows you to organize information and activities throughout your sales cycle, ensuring a smooth transition from initial offer to final closure.

By using this module, users can create, track, and categorize customer offers, monitor their status in real-time, and streamline follow-up processes to maximize conversion rates.

### **Managing Pipeline Boards**

1. To add a new board, click the [ <img src="/images/id/Tombol%20Papan%20(1).png" alt="Add Board" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] button.
2. The **[Add Pipeline]** dialog will prompt you to enter the Name, Description, and Stages of your pipeline.

To quickly replicate a deal, click the [ <img src="/images/id/Setting.png" alt="Settings" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] icon and select **Copy**. This streamlines the process for recurring or similar offers.

## **Financial Tracking**

### **Transactions**

Log payments or revenue milestones directly against a deal.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/tampilan%20transaksi%20penawaran.png"
  alt="Deal Transactions"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

Click **Add Transaction** to record payments, specifying the amount, date, payment method, and relevant attachments.

### **Expenses**

Track costs associated with closing a deal by clicking **Add Expense**. This ensures you can calculate more accurate profit margins for every opportunity.

---

## **Internal Notes & Logs**

### **Deal Notes**

Use the notes section for persistent information regarding the deal. While all users can add notes, editing and deletion are reserved for **Supervisors** to maintain audit trails.

### **Activity Logs**

The activity log provides a complete historical record of every change made to a deal, from creation to its final status (Won/Lost).

<Callout type="info" title="Tracking Logs">
Logs are essential for monitoring accountability and understanding the lifecycle of a deal, providing transparency across the entire sales team.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Maximize your deal management efficiency with these tips:

- Use clear and descriptive names for deals to differentiate similar opportunities.
- Keep deal values and closing dates updated for accurate sales forecasting.
- Move deals across pipeline stages in real-time to maintain an accurate project overview.
- Use comments to capture key negotiation points or customer feedback.
- Link every major action to a **Task** to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
- Track **Expenses** to see the true cost of customer acquisition.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues within the Deals module, follow these troubleshooting steps:

<Accordions>
<Accordion title="Account Verification">
- Confirm your account has the appropriate permissions to create or modify deals.
- Verify that your subscription hasn't expired or reached its deal limit.
- Ensure you are logged into the correct tenant/business profile.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="System & Performance">
- Check your internet connection for stability.
- Ensure you are using a supported, modern web browser.
- Refresh the pipeline view if new deals or updates do not appear immediately.
- Clear browser cache if UI elements or charts display incorrectly.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Data Entry Errors">
- Verify that all required fields in the deal form are completed.
- Ensure that monetary values do not contain unsupported characters.
- Check if any browser extensions (like ad-blockers) are interfering with the system scripts.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>
