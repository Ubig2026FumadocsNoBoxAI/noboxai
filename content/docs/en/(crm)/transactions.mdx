Accurate transaction logging is vital for generating reliable financial reports and evaluating the performance of your sales operations, ensuring that every payment milestone is systematically documented.

## **Adding a Transaction**

To log a new payment, click the **[Add Transaction]** button on the Transactions dashboard.

An **[Add Transaction]** dialog will appear, presenting a comprehensive data entry form.

### Transaction Form Fields

Complete all required fields within the form. Refer to the table below for standard examples:

| Field             | Description                        | Example                      |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Board             | Select the relevant pipeline board | November 2024 Deals          |
| Associated Deal   | Link to a specific opportunity     | PT Bimantara                 |
| Note              | Purpose of the transaction         | Down payment                 |
| Date              | Date of the transaction            | 28-11-2024                   |
| From              | Select the linked contact          | Sheila Syandana              |
| Payment Method    | Method used for the transaction    | Cash                         |
| Description       | Granular detail of the payment     | Initial project down payment |
| Currency & Amount | Select currency and set total      | Rp 500,000                   |
| Attachment        | Upload receipt or proof of payment | -                            |

Once the data is verified, click **[Save]** to finalize the transaction.

Ensure all figures are checked before saving to maintain the integrity of your financial reports.

## **Editing and Deleting Transaction Data**

### Editing Transactions

To update a payment record, select the target transaction from the list. Modify the necessary fields and click **[Save]**.

The updated information is synchronized across the CRM and reflected in all relevant deal summaries.

### Deleting Transactions

To remove a record, select the entry and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation prompt will appear to ensure the action is intentional.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
  Deleting transaction data is permanent. This action can significantly affect
  sales reports and commissions. Ensure that the deletion is truly necessary and
  approved before proceeding.
</Callout>

## **Financial Data Hygiene**

Effective transaction management requires consistent attention to detail. We recommend:

- **Real-time Updates**: Log payments as soon as they are received.
- **Categorization**: Ensure every transaction is linked to the correct board and deal.
- **Documentation**: Always upload supporting attachments for audit trails.
- **Formatting**: Use consistent naming conventions in the "Note" and "Description" fields.

Maintaining clean data simplifies your periodic financial evaluations and management reporting.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

To optimize your financial tracking:

- **Prerequisites**: Ensure the deal record exists before attempting to log a transaction.
- **Contextual Notes**: Use the notes field to clearly define if a payment is a deposit, installment, or final payoff.
- **Visual Proof**: Always attach digital receipts or bank transfer proofs for verification.
- **Double-Check**: Verify currency types and amounts before saving to avoid reconciliation issues.
- **Regular Audit**: Perform weekly checks to reconcile CRM transactions with actual bank statements.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience difficulties while using the Transactions module:

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Data Fails to Save">

    update.
