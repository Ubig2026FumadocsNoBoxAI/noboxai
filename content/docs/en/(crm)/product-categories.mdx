## **Adding a Product Category**

To create a new product category, navigate to the **[Product]** menu ➔ **[Product Categories]** ➔ **[Add Product Category]**.

An **[Add Product Category]** dialog will appear.

### Add Product Category Dialog View

### Add Product Category Form Fields

Complete the required information; for example:

| Field | Description    | Example     |
| ----- | -------------- | ----------- |
| Name  | Category Title | Electronics |

Ensure that category names are clear and accurately reflect the group of products they will contain.

Once the information is complete, click **[Save]** to store the new product category.

## **Editing and Deleting Category Data**

### Editing Product Categories

To modify a category, select the target record from the list. Update the name and click **[Save]** to apply the changes.

Updates are reflected immediately across all products currently assigned to that category.

### Deleting Product Categories

To remove a category, select the entry and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation prompt will appear to verify the action.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
  Category deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure that the category
  is no longer required before proceeding with the deletion.
</Callout>

## **How to Use Product Categories**

Follow these steps to assign a category to a specific product:

1. Open the **[Product]** menu and select an existing product record or create a new one.

2. Click on the **Category** input field; this will display a list of all previously created categories.

3. Select the appropriate category from the list and click **[Save]** to update the product data.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

To optimize your product organization:

- Use concise and descriptive category names.
- Avoid creating multiple categories with overlapping or similar names.
- Periodically review your category structure as your product line evolves.
- Remove obsolete categories to maintain a clean system interface.
- Ensure that the categories used align with your sales and inventory reporting needs.

</Tips>
