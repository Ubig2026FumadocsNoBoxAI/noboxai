## **Core Reporting Components**

| Component               | Function                                                                    |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Customer Sales Trends   | Tracks sales growth and volume based on customer-specific activities.       |

## **Detailed Component Breakdown**

- **Customer Sales Trajectory**
  Visualizes the rhythm of your sales cycle by tracking customer engagement—essential for identifying seasonal trends and high-value peaks.

- **Product Performance Momentum**
  Tracks the lifecycle of your product offerings. It helps identify which products are gaining popularity and which may require marketing adjustments or price revisions.

- **Top Companies & Product Leaders**
  Provides a leaderboard of your most valuable accounts and high-performing inventory items, allowing you to focus resources on your most profitable partnerships.

- **Deal Velocity Analysis**
  Monitors the movement of sales opportunities over time, identifying which pipeline stages are most effective and where potential bottlenecks may be occurring.

## **How to Analyze Trend Reports**

### Data Interpretation Guide

To gain strategic value from Trend Reports, consider these steps:

1. **Set the Analytical Focus**: Ensure the date range matches your review objective (e.g., quarterly growth or yearly performance).

2. **Identify Chart Patterns**: Look for consistent upward slopes or sudden dips that may indicate market shifts.

3. **Benchmarking Historical Data**: Compare current trends against previous years to determine if growth is organic or seasonal.

4. **Product-Market Fit**: Cross-reference top-performing products with customer trends to see what drives purchase behavior.

5. **Operational Health**: Use the Deal Stage chart to see if your sales team is closing deals faster or slower compared to previous periods.

### Utilizing Strategic Results

Trend Reports inform long-term business strategy, customer retention plans, and inventory expansion.

Leadership and supervisors should use this data to evaluate the effectiveness of historical decisions and to plan for upcoming market opportunities.

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues within the Trend Reports module:

- Verify that your internet connection is stable for high-volume data rendering.
- Refresh the browser page to update the real-time visual charts.
- Double-check the selected date range to ensure it contains active data.
- Confirm your account access level (analytical views may be role-restricted).
- Log out and log back in to synchronize your session with the latest server updates.
- Contact the support team if specific data points seem to populate incorrectly.
