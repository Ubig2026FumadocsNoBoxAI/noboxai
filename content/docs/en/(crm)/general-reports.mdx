The dashboard presents a visual summary of CRM performance using interactive charts and key performance indicators (KPIs).

From this page, users can evaluate sales health, transition between different deal pipelines, and access a holistic summary of daily operations.

### Key Data Insights

The dashboard is structured to highlight the most vital information for business stakeholders.

Data points include sales performance metrics, year-over-year comparisons, deal conversion ratios, recent task lists, and high-level summary reports for strategic analysis.

## **Core Reporting Components**

The General Reports dashboard is composed of several modules, each serving a specific analytical purpose:

| Component                 | Function                                                                    |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Sales Performance         | Tracks the team's deal results on a monthly basis.                          |
| Annual Sales Comparison   | Provides logic to compare sales performance across different years.         |
| Deal Board Conversion     | Visualizes how efficiently deals move through specific pipeline boards.     |
| Won Deals                 | Displays the total count of deals successfully closed.                      |
| Deals by Stage            | Categorizes current data based on its position in the sales pipeline.       |
| Summary Report            | Provides a consolidated overview of overall CRM health.                     |
| Sources                   | Identifies where deals and leads are originating from.                      |
| Lost Reasons              | Highlights the primary causes for failed deals to inform strategy changes.   |
| Recent Tasks              | Displays the most recent administrative and sales-related action items.     |
| Weighted Averages         | Calculates expected revenue based on deal stage probability.                |
| Daily Performance         | Tracks the day-to-day momentum of the sales team.                           |

## **Detailed Component Insights**

- **Sales Performance Tracking**
This chart visualizes transaction volume over time, helping leadership identify peak periods and growth trends.

- **Year-Over-Year Analysis**
Essential for strategic evaluation, this allows for direct comparisons of current results against historical benchmarks.

- **Deal Pipeline Conversions**
Monitors the flow of opportunities, identifying where leads are naturally moving forward or stalling.

- **Won Deals Breakdown**
Focuses on the final outcome of the sales process, attributing success to specific users and products.

- **Lost Reason Analysis**
Provides invaluable qualitative data on why opportunities are missed, allowing for rapid adjustment of sales tactics or product features.

## **How to Analyze General Reports**

### Data Interpretation Guide

To gain the most value from your General Reports, follow these analysis steps:

1. **Select the Timeframe**: Ensure the filters align with your specific audit cycle (Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly).

2. **Monitor Trends**: Look for consistent upward or downward movement in the Sales Performance charts.

3. **Benchmarking**: Compare data between different teams or regions to identify high-performing units.

4. **Conversion Audits**: Check the conversion percentages at each pipeline stage to optimize your sales funnel.

5. **Review Summaries**: Use the summary report to get a "pulse check" of the organization without getting lost in granular details.

6. **Actionable Outcomes**: Use these data points as the definitive foundation for strategic decision-making.

### Strategic Application

General Reports are the primary source for assessing overall sales health, the effectiveness of marketing spend, and the quality of customer relationship management.

Supervisors and executives should leverage these insights for performance reviews, quota planning, and identifying new business development opportunities.

## **Usage Tips**

To maximize the impact of your reporting:

- **Consistency**: Schedule a recurring time each week to review these reports.
- **Contextualize**: Cross-reference year-over-year data to account for seasonal business variations.
- **Goal Alignment**: Map these reports against your internal KPIs for real-time progress tracking.
- **Investigation**: When performance drops in a specific area, use more granular reports to find the root cause.
- **Data Integrity**: Remind your team to log all activities accurately, as the reports are only as good as the input data.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues with the General Reports module:
