## **Adding a Company Tag**

To create a new tag, click the **[Company Tags]** menu ➔ **[Add Company Tag]**.

An **[Add Company Tag]** dialog will prompt you to enter the name of the new tag.

### Add Tag Dialog View

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Tag%20Perusahaan.PNG"
  alt="Adding a New Company Tag"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

This form is used to input the new tag name that will be applied to your company records.

Once you have entered the tag name, click **[Save]** to store the data.

Ensure that the tag name is descriptive enough to facilitate easy identification and reuse later.

## **Editing and Deleting Tag Data**

### Editing Tags

To modify an existing tag, select the target entry from the list. Update the name and click **[Save]** to apply the changes.

The update will be reflected immediately and will remain available for all future company records.

### Deleting Tags

To remove a tag, select it from the list and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation dialog will appear.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Tag deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure that the tag is no longer required before proceeding with the deletion.
</Callout>

## **How to Use Company Tags**

Follow these steps to apply tags to your company records:

1. Navigate to the **[Companies]** menu and select an existing record or create a new one.

2. Click on the Tag column/field; this will trigger a dropdown of all previously created tags.

### Selecting Company Tags View

3. Select one or more tags from the list and click **[Save]** to update the company profile.

Using tags allows for more efficient categorization and retrieval of business data based on your specific requirements.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

To optimize your tagging strategy, consider these tips:

- Use clear, concise, and consistent tag names.
- Avoid over-tagging; too many tags can lead to confusion.
- Align tags with your specific business categories (e.g., Industry, Client Type, Region).
- Periodically audit your tags to ensure they remain relevant.
- Remove redundant or obsolete tags to keep the system clean.

</Tips>
