In the display, there are several sections, each with its own function. The explanation of these functions is as follows:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th width="185">Bagian</th>
      <th>Fungsi</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Target Value per Month</td>
      <td>

        the Won stage per month
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Target Deals per Month</td>
      <td>
        Information comparing the total of all offers with the total offers in
        the Won stage per month
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Monthly target based on Value</td>
      <td>
        Comparative information of all bid results with the total bid results in
        the Won stage per month based on users
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Monthly target based on Amount</td>
      <td>
        Comparison information of all total offers with total deals in Won stage
        per month based on users
      </td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
