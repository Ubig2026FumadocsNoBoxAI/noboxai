This tool assists users in managing and classifying products into specific groups, facilitating faster searching, more precise data filtering, and a more structured and efficient inventory management process within the system.

## **Adding a Product Tag**

To create a new tag, click the **[Product Tags]** menu ➔ **[Add Product Tag]**.

The **[Add Product Tag]** dialog will prompt you to enter the name of the tag.

### Add Tag Dialog View

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Tambah%20Tag%20Produk.png"
  alt="Adding a New Product Tag"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

This form is used to introduce new tags into your organizational workflow.

Once you have entered the tag name, click **[Save]** to store the data.

## **Editing and Deleting Tag Data**

### Editing Tags

To modify an existing tag, select the target entry from the list. Update the name as necessary and click **[Save]** to apply the changes.

Data updates are immediate and will be available for all future product assignments.

### Deleting Tags

To remove a tag, select the entry and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation dialog will appear to verify the action.

Click **[Yes]** to confirm or **[No]** to cancel.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Tag deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure that the tag is no longer being used by any active products before proceeding with the deletion.
</Callout>

## **How to Use Product Tags**

### Applying Tags to Products

1. Navigate to the **[Product]** menu and select an existing product record or create a new one.

2. Click on the **Tag** input field; this will trigger a dropdown of all previously created tags.

### Selecting Product Tags View

## **Filtering by Product Tags**

### The Utility of Tag Filtering

The **Tag Filter** is designed to isolate specific product data, displaying only records that match the selected tags.

This feature is particularly useful for teams managing large inventories, allowing them to find relevant products in seconds.

### How to Apply Tag Filters

In the **[Product]** menu, click on the **Tag Filter** and select the tags you wish to isolate. The product list will update automatically to show only the matching items.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

To maximize the effectiveness of your tagging system:

- Use clear, self-explanatory tag names.
- Avoid creating duplicate or nearly identical tags.
- Align your tagging structure with your broader product categories.
- Regularly review and update your tags to reflect changes in your product line.
- Maintain consistency in how tags are applied across the team.

</Tips>
