## **Core Reporting Components**

The Other Reports page focuses on two primary analytical pillars:

| Section               | Function                                                                   |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Achievement Over Time | Compares work results and goal fulfillment across different time periods.  |
| Activity Over Time    | Visualizes the total volume of system-level actions and updates over time. |

---

## **Detailed Component Breakdown**

- **Achievement Over Time**
  This component tracks specific user-generated outcomes (such as won deals or completed tasks) within a day, week, or month. It is essential for monitoring individual and team growth momentum.

- **Activity Over Time**
  Provides a log of collaborative actions, including group updates, the creation of internal notes, and changes to deal statuses. This ensures that managers can verify that CRM data is being maintained accurately and consistently.

## **How to Analyze Other Reports**

### Data Interpretation Guide

To gain actionable insights from Other Reports, follow these steps:

1. **Select the Analysis Period**: Ensure the chosen date range matches your review cycle (e.g., daily activity check or weekly achievement summary).

2. **Identify Achievement Patterns**: Look for steady growth or seasonal spikes in user productivity.

3. **Verify Operational Consistency**: Use the Activity chart to ensure the CRM is being utilized daily and that information is not being updated sporadically or in bulk at the end of a month.

4. **Monitor Significant Deviations**: Investigate sudden drops in activity or unusually high achievement spikes to understand the underlying causes.

5. **Employee Feedback**: Use these individual data points as a foundation for performance-based discussions and professional development.

### Utilizing Report Results

Other Reports are instrumental in assessing workforce productivity, identifying work patterns, and ensuring the quality of CRM activity management.

Managers and supervisors should use these reports for routine health checks, accountability monitoring, and building long-term performance optimization plans for the sales and support teams.

## **Usage Tips**

To optimize your review process:

- Use time-based filtering to see how new strategies impact team output.
- Link these activity logs into your broader internal performance metrics.
- Encourage a culture of real-time data entry so that the Activity chart remains accurate.

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while using the Other Reports menu:

- Refresh the system page to ensure all data points are loaded.
- Verify your internet stability, as large data ranges can require a robust connection.
- Confirm your account access level (Supervisor/Manager roles are typically required).
- Log out and log back in to sync your session with the latest server data.
- Clear your browser's persistent cache if charts appear distorted or empty.
- Contact the support team if specific activity logs seem to be missing.
