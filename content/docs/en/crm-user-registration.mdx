## **Preparation Before Registration**

### Technical Requirements

Before proceeding with registration, ensure the following are prepared:

- An active email address.
- Company or business information.
- A stable internet connection.
- A device that supports web access.

### Initial Check

Confirm that all required data is at hand before beginning the registration process.

---

## **Steps for CRM User Registration**

### Accessing the CRM Website

1.  Open your browser and navigate to the NoBox CRM website at:
    [https://crm.NoBox.AI](https://crm.NoBox.AI/)

2.  On the main page, click the **Register** button to create a new account.

### Filling Out the Registration Form

3.  Complete the registration form with the following information:

    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password
    - Company information
    - Other supporting data

4.  Once all information is correctly entered, click the **Register** button.

## **Account Email Verification**

### Email Delivery Process

After the registration process is complete, the system will automatically send a verification email.

### Verification Steps

Follow these stages:

1.  Open the inbox of the email address you registered.
2.  Locate the email from NoBox.AI.
3.  Click the verification link provided within the email.
4.  You will be redirected to the CRM login page.

### Verification Issues

If the email is not found, please check your spam or promotions folder.

---

## **Logging into the CRM System**

### Accessing the Login Page

Once your account has been successfully verified, you can immediately log in to the CRM system.

### Login Procedure

Follow these steps to log in:

1.  Go back to the NoBox CRM website.
2.  Enter the registered email address and password.
3.  Click the **Login** button.
4.  Wait for the system to process your authentication.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Here are some tips to streamline the CRM user registration process:

- Use an active professional email address for easier communication.
- Ensure business profile data is filled in completely so CRM features can function optimally.
- Store your login information in a secure location.
- Verify your email immediately after registering to prevent the account from expiring.
- Explore the dashboard menu after logging in to familiarize yourself with the system layout.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter obstacles during the Registration process, take the following steps:

- Use an email address that is active and frequently checked.
- Create a strong and secure password.
- Ensure company data is entered correctly.
- Store login information securely.
- Perform verification as soon as you receive the email.
