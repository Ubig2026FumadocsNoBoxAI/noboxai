## **Registration Steps**

Here are the stages to join as a NoBox.AI affiliate partner:

1. **Open Registration Portal**: Visit the registration page at [this link](https://crm.NoBox.AI/affiliate/authentication_affiliate/register).
2. **Fill in Personal Data**: Complete the registration form with valid and accurate information.
3. **Submit Registration**: Click the **[Register]** button to send your data to our system.
4. **Admin Verification**: Your registration will be reviewed by our admin team. Approval status will be sent via the registered email.
5. **Account Login**: Once approved, you can log in to the affiliate dashboard using the registered email and password.

## **Tips for Successful Registration**

- Use a primary email address that you access frequently so you don't miss registration status updates.
- Ensure the phone number entered is connected to WhatsApp to facilitate coordination with the affiliate support team.
- Save your username and password in a safe place so you don't encounter issues when logging in for the first time.
- Check your **Spam** or **Promotions** folder in your email if you haven't received an approval notification within 24 hours.

</Tips>

## **Handling Registration Issues**

If you encounter problems during registration, please follow these guidelines:

- **Email Already Registered**: Use the forgot password feature if you feel you have registered before, or use another email address.
- **Form Cannot Be Submitted**: Ensure all required fields are filled in and you have checked the agreement to the terms & conditions (if any).
- **Failed to Upload Profile Photo**: Ensure the image file size is not too large (maximum 2MB) and uses .jpg or .png format.
- **Account Not Approved for More Than 48 Hours**: Please contact the support team via ticket or available channels to inquire about your registration status.

</Kendala>
