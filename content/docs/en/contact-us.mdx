## **Tips to Get Fast Support**

To enable our team to provide solutions immediately, please ensure the following:

- **Include Tenant ID**: Include your tenant ID in every support email so we can instantly detect your account.
- **Attach Screenshots**: If experiencing technical issues, include images or short videos regarding the problem.
- **Clear Email Subject**: Use a specific email subject (e.g., "Login Failure - Tenant ID 0123") to facilitate our team's categorization.

</Tips>
