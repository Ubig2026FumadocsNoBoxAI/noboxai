NoBox.AI is committed to providing a healthy, safe, and trusted platform environment for all its users. We treat every abuse report very seriously and are dedicated to performing in-depth investigations into every violation reported to us.

If you find NoBox service usage that violates ethics or law, please report it immediately through the procedures below.

---

## **Prohibited Abuse Categories**

We divide violation types into several main categories to speed up the handling process:

### 1. Spam, Malware, and Phishing
It is strictly prohibited to use NoBox to send unwanted chain messages, distribute malicious software, or perform data theft attempts (*Phishing*) such as illegally requesting credit card details.

### 2. Hate, Violence, and Discrimination
Publication of content containing hate speech, racism, harassment, threats, or discrimination based on ethnicity or other categories is a major violation.

### 3. Child Exploitation (Zero Tolerance)
We have a zero-tolerance policy towards content that exploits children. Such content will be immediately deleted, the perpetrator's account will be permanently closed, and related data will be reported to the authorities.

### 4. Sexually Explicit Material
It is prohibited to publish or transmit adult content that is sexually explicit using our service infrastructure.

### 5. Personal and Confidential Information
It is prohibited to disseminate other people's personal data without their explicit permission, such as social security numbers, residency details, or confidential bank account numbers.

---

## **Reporting and Investigation Procedures**

After you submit a report, our security team will perform the following steps:
1.  **Report Validation**: Verifying the reporter's identity and the content considered to be in violation.
2.  **Enforcement Action**: Making decisions ranging from firm warnings, temporary account suspension, to permanent account closure.
3.  **Legal Follow-up**: Reporting criminal activities to law enforcement authorities if necessary.
