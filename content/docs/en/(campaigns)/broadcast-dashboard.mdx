This dashboard is highly useful for marketing managers or business owners to quickly evaluate delivery effectiveness and detect anomalies (such as high failure rates).

## **Analyzing Dashboard Data**

To maximize the use of this dashboard, you can perform the following:

1. **Monitor Success Rate**: Compare the **Sent** and **Failed** numbers. If the failure rate increases significantly, immediately check the stability of your channel connection.
2. **Observe Time Trends**: Use graphs to identify peak message delivery hours to adjust future marketing strategies.
3. **Click for Details**: If you see suspicious numbers (e.g., a queue that doesn't decrease), click on the widget to go directly to the message list page for further investigation.

---

<Tips>
## **Analysis Tips**

- **Clear the Queue**: If many messages remain in the queue for a long time, ensure your NoBox Desktop application is running smoothly.
- **Evaluate Failed Messages**: Immediately click on the "Failed" statistic to see if the failure was caused by inactive customer numbers or content issues (spam filter).
- **Use Date Filters**: Adjust the date range on the dashboard to view weekly or monthly campaign performance.
</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Statistic Numbers Don't Change**: Click the refresh button on your browser or ensure your internet connection is stable to fetch the latest data from the server.
- **Very High Failure Count**: Check if your message template contains words considered sensitive by WhatsApp/Telegram, or ensure your account is not being restricted (shadow banned).
- **Dashboard Doesn't Load Graphics**: Ensure your browser supports JavaScript and there are no content blocker extensions (ad-blockers) preventing dashboard scripts from loading.
</Kendala>
