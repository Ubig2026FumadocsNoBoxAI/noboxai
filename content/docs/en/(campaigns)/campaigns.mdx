**Message Templates** allow you to maintain brand consistency and drastically reduce composition time. You can build re-usable structures that pull dynamic data for deep personalization.

### **Template Interaction Features**

- **Template Library**: Browse and select from pre-approved messaging formats.
- **User Variables**: Automatically insert the sender's name or account details.
- **Company Branding**: Pull official business names, addresses, or support emails into the message footer.
- **Contact Tags**: Personalize greetings (e.g., "Hello, [First Name]") using data from the recipient's record.
- **External Data**: Map content directly from an uploaded Excel/CSV source.
- **Randomization Tools**: Insert random strings to vary message content, reducing the likelihood of being flagged as spam.
- **Spinners & Synonyms**: Use automated word-swapping to ensure no two messages sent in a bulk batch look identical.
- **Tracking Links**: Insert trackable URLs to monitor exactly who clicked on your promotion.
- **Split Messages**: Break long texts into multiple chat bubbles for a more natural, conversation-like feel.

## **Pre-Flight: Testing Your Campaign**

Always use the **Test Message Delivery** feature before broadcasting to hundreds of customers. This ensures that formatting, variables, and links are functioning as intended.

### **Executing a Test Send**

Click **[Test Send Message]**. The system will dispatch the campaign sample to your own linked number or email.

<ImageZoom
src="/images/id/Tes-Kirim-Pesan.png"
alt="Sending a Sample Campaign Message"
width={800}
height={450}
className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

**Checklist for Success:**
1. Is the text formatted correctly (bolding, spacing)?
2. Do the variable names pull the correct data (e.g., is "[Name]" replaced by an actual name)?
3. Does the link redirect to the correct landing page?
4. Do the image/video attachments load clearly?
5. Does the message look professional on the mobile version of the channel (WhatsApp/Telegram)?

## **Deployment and Batch Management**

### **Generating the Batch**

Under the **[Send Message]** tab, click **[Generate Messages]** (Buat Pesan).  
The system will cross-reference your contact list with your template to create unique message instances for every recipient. You can review the list of generated messages before they are processed.

## **Advanced Integration Features**

### **Bitly Tracking Links**
1. Integrate Bitly under **[Business Profile]** ➔ **[Integrations]**.
2. Enable the tracking feature in campaign setup.
3. Automated short-links will be generated for every recipient, allowing you to track individual click-through rates.

### **Excel Data Sources**
1. Upload your source file in the **[Data Source]** tab.
2. Select **[Map From] ➔ [Excel Columns]**.
3. Use the `[Data]` variable to call specific columns (e.g., "Hi [Data:CustomerName], your invoice for [Data:Amount] is ready").

<Tips>
## **Campaign Success Tips**

- **Personalization is Key**: Campaigns using at least two variables (e.g., Name and City) have significantly higher response rates.
- **A/B Testing**: Clone a campaign and try two different "Spinners" to see which phrasing drives more clicks.
- **Batching Strategy**: For large lists (>1000), schedule campaigns during off-peak hours to improve delivery success rates.
- **Audit Drafts**: Monthly, delete old drafts to keep your CRM performance fast and your workspace uncluttered.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Data Not Refreshing**: Toggle the 'Auto Refresh' switch or manually reload the page.
- **Messages Stuck in Processing**: Check if the sender account (WhatsApp/Telegram) is still authenticated and "Online" in the settings.
- **Excel Mapping Errors**: Ensure your Excel column headers match the "External ID" or data variable names used in your message body exactly.
- **Image Fails to Send**: Verify the attachment size; large files (>10MB) may time out on slower mobile connections.

</Kendala>
