Standardized templates also ensure brand consistency and reduce human error during high-pressure campaign cycles across multiple channels.

## **Creating a Message Template**

To build a new reusable format, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the **[Campaigns]** module ➔ **[Message Templates]** menu.
2. Click the **[Add Message Template]** button.
3. Populate the configuration fields in the resulting dialog.

### **Template Configuration Fields**

| Field            | Description                                                      | Example                                 |
| :--------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| **Name**         | The internal identifier for the template.                        | Order Confirmation - Retail            |
| **Channel**      | The intended communication medium.                               | WhatsApp                                |
| **Subject**      | The title or subject line (primarily for email/notification).    | Order Status Update                     |
| **Message Format**| The technical format of the content (Text, HTML, etc.).          | Text                                    |
| **Message Body** | Your template content. Supports variables and formatting.        | Hello, thank you for your order...       |
| **Attachments**  | Fixed files (PDFs, images) to be sent with this template.        | -                                       |

Once complete, click **[Save]** to commit the template to your library.

---

## **Managing Your Library**

### Editing Templates
Select a template from the list to modify its content or configuration. Click **[Save]** to apply changes. Updated templates are immediately available for use in any new campaign creation sessions.

### Deleting Templates
To remove a template, select it and click **[Delete]**, then confirm in the prompt. 

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Template deletion is permanent. If you delete a template, it will no longer be accessible for future campaigns. Ensure the content is truly obsolete before finalizing deletion.
</Callout>

## **Template Optimization Tips**

- **Descriptive Naming**: Use clear naming conventions (e.g., "Jan24_FlashSale_A") to help your team find the right content instantly.
- **Dynamic Variables**: Leverage personalization placeholders (like `[ContactName]`) to make generic templates feel personal to every recipient.
- **Channel Compliance**: Verify that your formatting (link style, image size) aligns with the specific rules of your chosen platform (e.g. WhatsApp's formatting tags).
- **Periodic Audits**: Update your templates quarterly to reflect changes in pricing, business hours, or brand voice.
- **Categorize by Goal**: Group templates internally as "Lead Follow-up," "Discount Offer," or "Transaction Confirmation" for better organization.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Template Not Appearing**: Ensure the "Channel" defined in your template matches the channel selected for your current campaign.
- **Save Failure**: Verify that all mandatory fields (Name, Channel, Message Body) have been populated.
- **Attachment Loading Errors**: Ensure the file size is under the 10MB limit and uses a standard format supported by the channel.
- **Formatting Glitches**: Avoid copy-pasting directly from Microsoft Word; use a plain text editor first to strip hidden formatting characters.
- **Non-Responsive Dialogs**: If the selection window doesn't open, refresh your browser or clear its local cache.

</Kendala>
