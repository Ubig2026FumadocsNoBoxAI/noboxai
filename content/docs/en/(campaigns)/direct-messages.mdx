This module is essential for urgent client communications, order verifications, or high-priority troubleshooting follow-ups.

## **Executing a Direct Dispatch**

To send a message, complete the operational fields in the form:

### Direct Message Field Requirements

| Field              | Description                                               | Example                                       |
| :----------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Contacts**       | Select one or more target individuals from your database. | Sheila Syandana                               |
| **Channel**        | Choose the communication medium (e.g., WhatsApp).         | WhatsApp                                      |
| **Account**        | Select the specific integrated sender account.            | s_whatsapp_primary                            |
| **Message Format** | Define content type (Text, Image, etc.).                  | Text                                          |
| **Shorten Link**   | Toggle the automated URL shortener and tracker.           | Inactive                                      |
| **Message Body**   | The personalized content you wish to deliver.             | Hello, your order #1234 is being processed... |
| **Attachment**     | Upload specific supporting documents or images.           | -                                             |

Once the data is populated, click **[Send Now]** to initiate the delivery process.

---

## **Post-Dispatch Monitoring**

After clicking send, you do not need to remain on this page. The system handles the background processing. You can track the real-time delivery status of these individual messages through the **[Broadcast Messages]** menu.

<Tips>
## **Operational Tips**

- **Search Efficiency**: Use the search bar in the contact field to rapidly locate specific customer names from large databases.
- **Account Health**: Always ensure your sender account status is "Online" before initiating a high-priority direct message.
- **Hyper-Personalization**: Since these are direct messages, use specific details like names or previous purchase history to increase engagement.
- **Proofread**: Double-check for typos in the message body before sending, as direct messages cannot be edited after dispatch in most channels.
- **Visual Support**: Attach invoices, catalogs, or transaction proofs directly to provide more value to the recipient.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Delivery Failure**: Verify that the recipient's phone number is correctly formatted and that your sender account is properly authenticated.
- **Empty Channel List**: If no channels appear, check your business profile integration settings to re-link your accounts.
- **Interface Latency**: If the "Send Now" button appears unresponsive, refresh the page and check your local internet stability.
- **Contact Search Issues**: Ensure the specific contact has been successfully imported or created in the master NoBox.AI Contact database.
- **Attachment Errors**: Confirm the file size is within the allowed limits of the target channel (e.g., WhatsApp attachment limits).

</Kendala>
