The **Broadcast Messages** menu in NoBox.AI is used to monitor the output of message sending from all your promotion data. This feature allows you to track each message individually, from its status in the queue, through the delivery process, to successful or failed delivery.

With this menu, you can ensure that your promotional messages reach your audience correctly and analyze any issues that may occur during delivery.

## **Viewing Message Details**

To see the detailed information of a specific message, follow these steps:

1. Click on one of the message entries you wish to inspect.
2. A detailed information panel or dialog will appear.
3. Detailed information includes:
   - **Recipient**: The target phone number or username.
   - **Content**: The text or media content sent.
   - **Time Logs**: Records of when the message was created, started processing, and finished sending.
   - **Failure Reason**: If the status is failed, a technical reason for why the message did not arrive will usually be displayed.

<Tips>
## **Monitoring Tips**

- **Filter by Status**: Use the filter feature to focus only on "Failed" messages to perform manual resending if necessary.
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Monitor the "In Process" status to ensure the delivery speed meets your expectations.
- **Check Account Validity**: If many messages have a "Failed" status, immediately check the connection status of your WhatsApp or Telegram account in the Accounts menu.
</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Status Remains In Queue**: This usually happens if the system queue is very busy or your NoBox Desktop is inactive. Ensure the Desktop application is in a "Ready" state.
- **High Failure Rate**: Ensure the target numbers use the correct international format (e.g., 628xxx) and your sender account is not being blocked by the provider.
- **Out of Sync Data**: If the status on the dashboard differs from your device, try clicking the refresh or data sync button on that page.
</Kendala>
