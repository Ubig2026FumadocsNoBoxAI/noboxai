## **Message Detailed Information**

The dashboard provides granular data for every message in the broadcast system, including:

- **Message/Campaign Name**: Identifies the source campaign or promotion for the specific message.
- **Channel & Sender Account**: Displays the communication medium used (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) and the specific integrated account used for dispatch.
- **Target Contact**: Lists the recipient's name or destination identification details.
- **Current Status**: Provides a real-time update on where the message exists within the delivery lifecycle.
- **Timestamp**: Displays the exact moment the message was sent or its designated future schedule.

## **Operational Control and Management**

The Broadcast Messages module provides tools for deep evaluation and rapid correction of your communication activities.

### Accessing Message Details

For complex troubleshooting or to review individual content:

1. Locate the specific message record within the dashboard table.
2. Click directly on the row to expand the information window.
3. Review the **Message Body**, **Attachments**, **Sent/Scheduled Timestamps**, and the complete **Process History Log**.

### Recovering from Failures

If you identify a significant number of "Failed" statuses, use the **[Failures to Queue]** feature. This allows you to retry the broadcast for those specific failed recipients without the need to recreate the entire marketing campaign from scratch.

<Tips>
## **Operational Best Practices**

- **Pre-Launch Audit**: Verify that your sender accounts are "Online" and authenticated before initiating a massive broadcast.
- **Target Accuracy**: Regularly validate your contact lists to minimize failures caused by inactive phone numbers or disabled accounts.
- **Phased Deployment**: For mission-critical communications, perform a small sample send (10-20 people) to verify formatting before dispatching to thousands.
- **Queue Vigilance**: Monitor the "Queued" volume occasionally to ensure throughput is meeting your intended marketing timeline.
- **Smart Filtering**: Use the dashboard filters to quickly isolate specific campaigns or check only for "Failed" messages during campaign reviews.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Persistent 'Processing' Status**: Check your internet connection or the status of your integrated WhatsApp/Telegram account; a persistent processing state often indicates a disconnection.
- **Missing Data**: Refresh the page or toggle the "Auto-Refresh" switch if the table fails to populate new entries.
- **Batch Failures**: If every message in a batch is failing, investigate the health of the specific "Sender Account" or channel gateway integration.
- **Dashboard Lag**: For extremely high-volume accounts, ensure you are using a modern browser version and clear your cache to maintain interface responsiveness.

</Kendala>
