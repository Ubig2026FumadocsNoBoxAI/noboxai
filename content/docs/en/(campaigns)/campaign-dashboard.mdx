The **Campaign Dashboard** is an information hub that presents a summary of all promotional campaigns you have created. This page is designed to facilitate decision-making by presenting cumulative data on how many campaigns are still in draft, currently running, or successfully completed.

Unlike the Broadcast Dashboard, which focuses on individual message details, the Campaign Dashboard focuses on your promotion projects as a whole.

## **How to Read Campaign Data**

Users can analyze promotion effectiveness through several indicators on this dashboard:

1. **Completion Efficiency**: See if many campaigns are stuck in the "Process" status. If so, there might be an issue with account delivery speed or the volume of targets might be too large.
2. **Promotion Planning**: If the "Draft" number is high, it indicates many campaigns are planned but not yet executed.
3. **Target vs. Realization Analysis**: On the campaign detail widget, you can see the comparison between the number of target contacts and the number actually successfully sent.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

- **Execute Drafts Promptly**: Monitor the "Draft" card regularly so that no promotional plans are delayed or miss their intended schedules.
- **Evaluate Completed Campaigns**: Click on the "Done" statistic to review the final reports of those promotions and compare them with sales targets.
- **Use Clear Naming**: When creating a promotion, give it a specific name so it is easily identifiable in the "Latest Campaigns" list.
</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Campaign Doesn't Move to Done**: Ensure all messages in the queue have been sent (check the Broadcast Messages menu). A campaign will only reach "Done" status once all targets have been processed.
- **Data Doesn't Appear**: Ensure you have sufficient access rights (role) to view the summary of all campaigns. Contact your administrator if the dashboard appears empty.
- **Slow Synchronization**: If you have just started a large campaign, the dashboard may take a few moments to update the real-time delivery statistics symbols.
</Kendala>
