**Mail Queue** (Antrean Email) is a specialized monitoring module in NoBox.AI used to track the progress and health of promotional emails or notification dispatches. Through this interface, you can monitor every lifecycle stage of the emails processed by the system.

This feature ensures that high-volume email campaigns are dispatched on schedule and provides technical visibility to identify and resolve delivery bottlenecks or failures.

## **Understanding Email Delivery Statuses**

The queue table uses several key indicators to define the condition of your email transmission:

| Status Indicator | Technical Function                                                                 |
| :--------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **In Queue**     | The email is waiting for its turn to be picked up and processed by the mail server. |
| **Sent**         | The email has successfully left the system and was accepted by the recipient's mail provider. |
| **Failed**       | Delivery was unsuccessful due to address errors, server downtime, or rate limits. |
| **Retries**      | Displays the number of automated attempts the system has made to re-send a delayed email. |

## **Workflow and Queue Management**

Admins can take specific actions on items within the queue to ensure communication efficiency and troubleshoot persistent failures.

### Investigating Failed Emails
If an email remains in the **"Failed"** state, immediately verify the recipient's email address in the contact database. In many cases, simple typos or invalid domains (e.g., "gmaill.com") are the root cause. For systematic failures, check your SMTP server credentials and current daily volume limits.

<Tips>
## **Queue Management Tips**

- **Retry Monitoring**: Keep an eye on the "Retries" column. If an email has multiple retries without success, it may be time to manually intervene or contact the recipient via another channel.
- **SMTP Health**: Ensure your SMTP integration or third-party email provider is "Active" and has sufficient daily quotas remaining for large broadcasts.
- **Historical Analysis**: Use the date filters to audit previous email campaign performance and identify peaks in delivery failures.
- **Data Hygiene**: Immediately prune or correct contact records that result in "Hard Bounces" (invalid addresses) to maintain a high domain sender reputation.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Email Traffic Jam**: If messages are piling up in the queue without moving, check your outgoing SMTP server connection settings.
- **Persistent Failure status**: Verify if your email provider's daily sending limits have been exceeded.
- **Stale Dashboard Data**: Click the browser refresh button to force-sync the latest status updates from the server.
- **Spam Folder Issues**: Monitor your domain reputation and ensure your email content follows anti-spam best practices (avoiding "spammy" keywords or all-caps subject lines).
- **Interface Rendering**: Clear your browser cache or update your browser version if the queue table layout appears broken or misaligned.

</Kendala>
