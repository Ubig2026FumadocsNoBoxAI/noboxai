## **GDPR Compliance**

**GDPR** (*General Data Protection Regulation*) is a pan-European regulation that requires businesses to protect the personal data and privacy of EU citizens or residents in every data processing process.

NoBox welcomes GDPR as a force for strengthening the privacy awareness that has been inherent in it since the beginning. NoBox's offerings have privacy features that fully comply with GDPR data protection principles.

## **Tips for Maintaining Compliance**

For corporate users or agents, you can help maintain compliance by:

- **Data Audit**: Clean up contact data that is no longer active or does not have sending permission.
- **Contact Permission**: Ensure you have written consent before adding customer contacts to promotional campaign stacks.
- **Transparency**: Provide clear information to your customers regarding the purpose of collecting their profile data.

</Tips>
