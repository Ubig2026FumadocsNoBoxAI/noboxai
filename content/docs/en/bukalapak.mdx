## **Add Account**

Ensure you have subscribed to Bukalapak Automation. To add a new account, please open the **NoBox Desktop application ➔ [Automations] ➔ [Add Account]**. The **[Add Account]** dialog will appear.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Account%20Bukalapak.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

| Field   | Description                               | Example              |
| ------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Channel | The channel you select                    | Bukalapak           |
| Name    | Your account name                         | Bukalapak CS Fitria |
| Notes   | Provide notes about the account           | -                   |
| Status  | Information whether the account is active | Active              |

Once you have completed the fields, click **[OK]** to save the account you just created.

## **Configuration**

After adding a new account, you only need to open the Bukalapak account you created through a PC/laptop.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Login%20Bukalapak.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

- Open the NoBox Desktop application.
- The newly created Account will appear in NoBox Desktop. Then Login to the Bukalapak account to link it with your Bukalapak.
- Ensure in the **[Logs]** tab there is the information **"Bukalapak Automation is ready"**.
- In the **[Outbox]** tab, ensure the status is **[Worker On]**, then the account is ready to use.

---

## **Auto Login**

You can also use the auto login feature in NoBox Desktop by clicking the **[Settings]** menu. Fill in your Bukalapak Account username and password, then click **[OK]**.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Auto%20Login%20Bukalapak.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

Your username and password will be directly set on the Bukalapak login page. Ensure in the logs tab there is the information **“Bukalapak Automation is ready”** and in the **[Outbox]** tab the status is **[Worker On]**. Once done, the account is ready for use.

## **How to Use a Bukalapak Account**

You can use a Bukalapak Account to send campaigns as follows:

1. Open the website [id.NoBox.AI](https://id.NoBox.AI/) then click the **[Campaigns]** module and click the **[Campaigns]** menu.

2. Select a campaign or create a new one, choose the **[Bukalapak]** Channel, select the **[Bukalapak Jasmine]** Account you just created, and ensure the Bukalapak account is active.

3. Ensure the campaign data is filled in correctly and click process for that campaign.

4. You can monitor the campaign delivery from NoBox Desktop in the **[Outbox]** tab.

5. Additionally, you can also see the campaign results in the **[Inbox]** menu so you can directly monitor campaign results and reply to chats directly with your campaign targets.

## **Pausing Campaign Processes**

This feature can facilitate you in pausing the campaign sending process. The steps are as follows:

1. In the **[Outbox]** tab, click the **[Worker]** button and ensure the worker status is **[OFF]**.

2. Then the sending process will be paused.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Jeda%20Bukalapak.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

3. To continue the sending process, click the **[Worker]** button and ensure the worker status is **[ON]**.

4. Then the sending process will continue.

## **Deactivating a Bukalapak Account**

This feature serves to reduce the RAM usage of the NoBox Desktop application on your device.

To deactivate an account, select one of your Bukalapak accounts, then uncheck the status section. Once done, click **[Save]** to save those changes.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Nonaktif%20Bukalapak.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

You can check the NoBox Desktop application; the Bukalapak account that was just deactivated will not appear.

---

## **Editing and Deleting Accounts**

To edit an account, please open and log in to the website [id.NoBox.AI](https://id.NoBox.AI/), click the **[Accounts]** menu. Select the account you want to edit, then edit your data and click **[Save]** to save the changes.

To delete an account, select the account to be deleted, then click **[Delete]**. A confirmation notification will appear asking if you want to delete that account. If yes, click **[Yes]**, if not click **[No]**.
