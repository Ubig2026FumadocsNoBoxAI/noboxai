The Billing menu is used to manage all transaction processes in NoBox.AI, from purchasing packages, selecting subscription durations, to payment confirmation. Through this menu, users can also view active package status, remaining quota, and transaction history.

This menu is designed to make the transaction process easy, whether through automatic payments or manual transfers. Make sure each step is completed correctly so the service can be activated without issues.

## **How to Place an Order**

Below are the complete steps to purchase a package in NoBox.AI:

**1. Go to the Billing Menu**

Click the **[Billing]** menu. The system will display the Billing dashboard containing active package information, remaining quota, and available subscription packages.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Tampilan%20Billing.png"
  alt="Billing Menu Display"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

At the top of the page, you will see a summary of the current package:

| Component | Description |
|----------|------------|
| **Package Details** | <img src="/images/id/Package%20Details%20Billing.png" className="w-auto h-auto mx-auto" /> Displays current package information, including name and active period |
| **Monthly Active Users** | <img src="/images/id/Monthly%20Active%20Users%20Billing.png" className="w-auto h-auto mx-auto" /> Shows the number of active users per month used from the total quota |
| **Human Agents** | <img src="/images/id/Human%20Agents%20Billing.png" className="w-auto h-auto mx-auto" /> Displays the number of internal agent accounts used |
| **AI Responses** | <img src="/images/id/AI%20Responses%20Billing.png" className="w-auto h-auto mx-auto" /> Shows the number of AI responses used from the total quota |

This information helps you determine whether you need to upgrade your package.

**2. Choose Subscription Duration**

In the middle section, there are subscription duration options:

- **Monthly** → suitable for short-term or trial use

Click one of the duration tabs before selecting a package.

---

**3. Choose a Subscription Package**

After selecting a duration, you will see several package options such as:

- **Basic**
- **Pro**
- **Expert**

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Paket%20Billing.png"
  alt="Billing Packages"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

Each package differs in:

- Subscription price  
- Number of AI Responses  
- Number of Monthly Active Users  
- Number of Human Agents  
- Additional features such as WhatsApp, Telegram, CRM integration, and more  

Choose the package that best suits your needs.

---

**4. Click [Buy Package]**

After selecting your desired package, click the **[Buy Package]** button at the bottom of the package card to start the purchase process.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Buy%20Package%20Billing.png"
  alt="Buy Package"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

**5. Confirm Purchase**

The system will display a purchase confirmation dialog.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Buy%20Billing.png"
  alt="Confirm Purchase"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

At this stage:

- Review the selected package and duration  
- Ensure there are no mistakes  
- Click **[Buy]** to proceed  

---

**6. Choose Payment Method**

Next, select an available payment method.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Payment%20Billing.png"
  alt="Payment Method"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

Choose the method that is most convenient for you.

---

**7. Complete Payment**

After selecting a payment method:

- The system will display an invoice or payment details  
- Complete the payment according to the specified amount  

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Transaksi%20Billing.png"
  alt="Transaction"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

<Callout type="warn" title="Important Information">
  The payment deadline is **24 hours** after clicking the **[Buy]** button. If the deadline is exceeded, the transaction will be automatically canceled by the system.
</Callout>

---

After making the payment, scroll to the **Recent Transactions** section at the bottom of the page.

If the status is **Paid**, the package will be activated automatically.

## **Types of NoBox.AI Subscription Packages**

NoBox.AI provides several subscription options that can be adjusted based on user needs.

### **1. Monthly**

Suitable for:

- New users  
- Short-term needs  
- Small to medium usage  

**Advantages:**

- Flexible  
- Suitable for trials  

---

### **2. Half-Yearly (6 Months)**

Suitable for:

- Growing businesses  
- Users who want better pricing  

**Advantages:**

- More economical than monthly  
- No monthly payments  

---

### **3. Yearly**

Suitable for:

- Intensive business use  
- Long-term users  

**Advantages:**

- Most cost-effective  
- No frequent renewals  
- Stable for long-term use

<Tips>
## **Transaction Security Tips**

- Use the reference number provided in the invoice  
- Ensure the transfer amount matches exactly  
- Keep proof of payment  
- Pay before the deadline (24 hours)  

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Service not active** → Ensure payment proof has been submitted  
- **Invoice not appearing** → Refresh page or check email (including Spam)  
- **Payment failed** → Check balance or payment method  
- **Incorrect transfer** → Contact support immediately with proof  
</Kendala>
