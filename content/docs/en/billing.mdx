## **How to Place an Order**

Steps to subscribe to NoBox.AI products are as follows:

1. Go to the **[Product]** page on your dashboard.
2. Search for and select the product or plan you need.
3. Click the **[Order]** button to start the purchase.
4. You will be automatically directed to the **Invoice** page containing cost details and billing number.

## **Payment Methods**

We provide flexible options to settle your bills:

### **1. Online Payment**
You can make instant payments through the PayPal platform using your PayPal balance or the *Smart Checkout* feature. Online payments will activate your service automatically without requiring manual confirmation.

### **2. Offline Payment (Bank Transfer)**
Specifically for Indonesian users, we provide local accounts (BCA and Mandiri). If using this method, it is **mandatory** to send proof of transfer via the ticket system.

---

## **Transfer Payment Confirmation**

After performing a bank transfer, please upload proof of payment through the following steps:

1. Click the **[Back to Portal]** button and navigate to the **[Support]** menu.
2. Select the **[Open Ticket]** sub-menu.
3. Complete the ticket form according to your transaction details.

### **Payment Confirmation Dialog Fields**

| Field | Description | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Subject** | Ticket summary. | BCA Payment Confirmation |
| **Department** | Ticket category. | Billing |
| **Priority** | Level of importance. | Medium |
| **Ref No**| Your invoice number. | INV-000220 |
| **Ticket Body** | Brief description. | PRO package payment |
| **Attachments** | Photo of transfer proof. | *Image File/Payment Proof* |

After the ticket is sent, the ticket status will be **OPEN**. Once the admin has confirmed, the status changes to **CLOSED** and your service will be active.

## **Transaction Security Tips**

Ensure your transaction goes smoothly with these tips:

- Use the reference number or unique code stated on the invoice when performing the transfer.
- Always save physical receipts or bank transaction screenshots as backup proof.
- Ensure the amount you transfer is exactly the same down to the last digit.
- Send payment proof immediately after the transaction is complete for faster verification.
- Use online payment methods for more instant service activation.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting Billing**

If you experience issues related to billing or payment, follow these steps:

- **Service Not Active After Transfer**: Ensure you have sent the payment proof ticket in the Billing department.
- **Invoice Not Appearing**: Refresh the page or check the Spam folder in your registered email.
- **Online Payment Method Failed**: Check your balance or card limit, or contact PayPal.
- **Wrong Transfer Amount**: Immediately contact our support team via a [Support Ticket](https://crm.NoBox.AI/clients/tickets) by attaching proof of the transfer error.

</Kendala>
