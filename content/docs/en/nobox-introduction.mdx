## **Primary Features of NoBox Desktop**

The NoBox Desktop ecosystem is built upon two complementary pillars designed to accelerate your business growth:

### **1. Automation (Message Broadcasting)**
This feature is used to conduct promotional broadcasts to modern channels such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, Instagram, and marketplace platforms. The system ensures your messages reach target prospects with a high degree of personalization.

### **2. Extractor (Data Harvesting)**
A tool for building unlimited lead databases from over 700+ business directory sites and classified ad platforms, including Google Maps, OLX, Yellow Pages, and many more.

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while running NoBox Desktop, please perform the following checks:

- **Application Fails to Open**: Ensure cross-platform internet stability and that your device meets minimum system requirements.
- **Data Not Syncing to Web**: Try logging out and back into the desktop application to refresh the cloud connection session.
- **Internal Browser Lag**: Clear application cache via the settings menu or close unused extraction tabs.
- **Account Connection Lost**: Re-scan (QR scan) or update the session for your social media accounts.

</Kendala>
