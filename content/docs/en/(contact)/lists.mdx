**Lists** (List) is a fundamental grouping module in NoBox.AI designed to categorize and organize your customer records into specific datasets based on your unique business requirements.

This menu enables users to perform precise audience segmentation for promotional campaigns, mass communications, or category-based customer relationship management. With the Lists feature, you can build specialized outreach targets, add contacts via multiple integration methods, and manage segment memberships with high flexibility.

## **Creating a New List**

1. Navigate to the **[Contacts]** module ➔ **[Lists]** ➔ click **[Add List]**.
2. An **[Add List]** dialog will launch. Enter the title for your organizational segment.

## **Managing Segment Records**

### Editing Lists
To rename a list, select the entry and modify the title in the form. Click **[Save]** to apply. This update will take effect across all individual records currently associated with the list.

### Deleting Lists
To remove a segment grouping entirely, select the list and click **[Delete]**. Confirm the action in the prompt.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting a list only removes the grouping/container. The actual contact records within the list remain intact in your primary database. However, ensure the list is not currently being used by an active or scheduled campaign before deletion.
</Callout>

## **Methods for Adding Contacts to Lists**

NoBox.AI provides multiple workflows to populate your lists, depending on your source data:

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/List%20Tambah%20Kontak.png"

### Option 1: Add from Primary Contacts
Use this method to pull existing records from your main NoBox.AI database into a specialized list.

1. Select the target list from the dashboard.
2. Click **[Add Contact]** ➔ **[Add from Contacts]**.
3. Choose one or multiple entries from your master database.
4. Click **[OK]** to finalize the enrollment.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/tambah%20dari%20kontak.png"
  alt="Enrolling Existing Database Records into Segments"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

### Option 2: Add from Promotion Targets
This advanced method allows you to collect contacts who were targeted in previous campaigns. This is excellent for building "Retargeting Lists" or "Engaged Lead Lists."

1. Select the target list.
2. Click **[Add Contact]** ➔ **[Add from Promotion Target]**.
3. Select the specific historical campaign whose leads you wish to harvest.
4. Click **[OK]** to transfer the contacts into your segment.

## **Clearing List Memberships**

The **Remove All Contacts from List** feature allows you to empty a segment container instantly. This is useful for resetting seasonal lists (e.g., "Monthly Giveaway") for a fresh set of participants.

1. Select the list you wish to empty.
2. Click **[Remove All Contacts]**.
3. Confirm the mass removal in the dialog box.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/List%20Hapus%20Kontak.png"

  width={800}
  height={450}

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Emptying a list only removes the *link* between the contact and that list. It does not delete the customer’s profile from your system.
</Callout>

## **Lists as Campaign Targets**

Lists are the primary "Targeting" mechanism for the NoBox.AI Promotions engine. Instead of selecting individual contacts one by one, users simply select a pre-defined List. This significantly reduces human error and ensures your marketing messages reach the intended audience segment with a single click.

<Tips>
## **Segmentation Best Practices**

- **Contextual Categorization**: Group contacts by geography, interests, or lifecycle status (e.g., "Surabaya VIP," "Active Resellers 2024") for hyper-relevant messaging.
- **Workflow Efficiency**: Use the **[Add from Contacts]** feature to rapidly organize large databases into manageable sub-segments.
- **Retargeting Strategy**: Use **[Add from Promotion Targets]** to build a secondary list of people who received a specific offer, allowing you to send a follow-up discount only to that specific group.
- **Naming Conventions**: Maintain a consistent naming system across your team to avoid overlapping or duplicate lists.
- **Data Hygiene**: Periodically use **[Remove All Contacts]** to clear out temporary lead lists once their lifecycle is complete.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Membership Delay**: If contact counts don't update immediately after an addition, refresh your browser to pull the latest synchronized totals.
- **Selection Sensitivity**: Ensure you have accurately selected the target contacts or campaigns when using bulk addition tools.
- **Bandwidth Latency**: If adding hundreds of contacts to a list at once, maintain a stable internet connection to ensure the server-side process completes successfully.
- **Permission Denials**: If edit or delete buttons are inactive, verify that your account has the correct "Supervisor" or "Administrator" privileges.
- **Rendering Issues**: Clear your browser cache if the List table or dialog boxes appear broken or misaligned.

</Kendala>
