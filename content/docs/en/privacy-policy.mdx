Your privacy is our primary priority. At NoBox.AI, we adhere to the principle of full transparency regarding how we collect, use, and protect your data. This policy is designed to provide a clear understanding without complex legal language regarding your privacy rights on our platform.

We are committed to never selling your data or generating revenue from third-party advertising.

---

## **Scope of Privacy Policy**

This policy applies to the entire NoBox.AI ecosystem, including:
- Official website and sub-domains.
- NoBox Desktop application.
- Official NoBox integration services and mobile applications.
- Interactions through support forms and our official emails.

---

## **Policy Structure**

To facilitate navigation, this policy is divided into three main sections:

### Section I – Information Collected by NoBox
Discusses how we collect basic data about site visitors, potential customers, and service users when you register or contact our support team.

### Section II – Information We Process on Your Behalf
Discusses the handling of data/databases you upload into the NoBox system (such as contact lists and chat history) and how we maintain the confidentiality of that data as a data processor.

### Section III – General Provisions
Covers global security topics, data retention duration, and how we inform you of future policy changes.

---

## **Our Primary Commitments**

We only collect the minimal information necessary to support product functionality. Any form of additional data collection will always be based on your explicit consent. We ensure that conflicts of interest between data collection and advertising will never occur in the NoBox.AI system.

<Tips>
## **User Privacy Notes**

- **Data Control**: You have full rights to edit, download, or delete your personal data through the account dashboard.
- **Session Transparency**: All login activities are monitored to detect unauthorized access from unknown locations.
- **Policy Updates**: We will notify you via email if significant changes occur in the way we handle your data.

</Tips>

## **Privacy Questions**

If you have specific concerns or questions regarding the handling of your data by NoBox.AI:

- **Contact Privacy Officer**: Send an email to [privacy@NoBox.AI](mailto:privacy@NoBox.AI).
- **Delete Account**: If you wish to exercise the "Right to be Forgotten", you can request permanent account deletion via a support ticket.
- **Data Audit**: You can request a copy of your personal data stored on our servers in a machine-readable format.

</Kendala>
