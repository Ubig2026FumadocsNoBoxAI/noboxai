This store system is directly integrated with the affiliate dashboard to ensure every order is accurately recorded into the respective partner's commission.

## **Monitoring Order Status**

You can monitor order progress through the **[Order List]** menu. Here is the meaning of each order status:

| Status | Explanation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Processing** | Payment is being validated or the order is being prepared. |
| **Confirm** | Order has been confirmed and the service is being processed. |
| **Finish** | Transaction is complete and product service is fully active. |
| **Canceled** | Order was canceled (either by the customer or the system). |

## **Tips for Shopping at the Affiliate Store**

- Ensure you are logged in or have registered a customer account so your order history is neatly saved.
- Double-check the **Billing Address** fields to ensure invoices are sent to the correct address.
- Use the category search feature to find promo packages that are often offered by affiliate partners.
- If performing a manual transfer, immediately attach proof of payment through the portal provided to speed up the confirmation process.

</Tips>

## **Handling Order Issues**

If you encounter problems while transacting at the Store, follow these guidelines:

- **Failed to Add Product to Cart**: Refresh the page or ensure the item is not in "Out of Stock" status.
- **Invoice Not Appearing After Order**: Check the **Order List** menu; if the order is there, you can reopen the invoice from there.
- **Incorrect Product Selection**: While the order is still in **Processing** status, you can manually cancel it via the **[Cancel Order]** button and place a new order.
- **Product Not Active After Payment**: Please be patient for the manual verification process (max 1x24 hours) or contact your affiliate partner support.

</Kendala>
