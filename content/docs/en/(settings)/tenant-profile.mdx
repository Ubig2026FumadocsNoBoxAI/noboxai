**Business Profile** (Profil Usaha) is a central management hub in NoBox.AI that stores comprehensive information about your company and serves as the primary interface for managing various business operational settings.

This menu allows users to configure company information, manage team members and their roles, set up inbox preferences, connect third-party integrations, and define operating hours with automatic responses. It serves as the single source of truth for all organizational configurations within the NoBox.AI platform.

## **Information Tab Components**

The Information tab is divided into sub-sections, each with a specific administrative purpose:

- **General**
Contains fundamental company data such as the business name, logo, official email, contact phone number, and legal identity records. This information acts as your company's identifier across the entire system.

- **Address**
Displays and manages your official business location, including city, province, country, and postal code. This data is critical for official documentation, invoicing, and customer location referencing.

- **Default Settings**
Configures the primary communication channel and account used for outgoing promotional broadcasts. By setting a default, users avoid manually selecting channels for every new campaign, significantly improving workflow efficiency.

- **Auto-Resolve (Selesai Otomatis)**
Toggles a feature that automatically marks active conversations as "Resolved" after a defined period of inactivity. This helps keep the inbox clean without manual intervention.

- **Auto-Archive (Arsip Otomatis)**
Automates the movement of "Resolved" conversations into the system archive. This ensures the live workspace stays focused on active interactions while safely storing historical context.

- **Additional Notes**
An optional space for internal documentation regarding company data or specific administrative reminders.

After making any changes to these sections, click **[Save]** to commit the updates.

### Account Role Definitions

NoBox.AI utilizes specialized roles to ensure security and operational focus:

| Role           | Technical Description                                                                                                                                                                 |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Owner          | The highest level of access. Owners have unrestricted control over all features, billing, and the ability to manage or remove any other team member.                                  |
| Manager        | High-level management access. Managers can access all primary features except the ability to modify the Owner's role or core billing details.                                       |
| Member         | Standard operational access. Members can use most CRM and campaign features but cannot access account-wide settings or Business Profile configurations.                             |
| Chat Supervisor | Specialized inbox oversight. Supervisors can monitor all conversations (Assigned or Unassigned), view agent performance reports, and filter chats based on team permissions.      |
| Chat Agent     | Focused delivery execution. Agents can only see, read, and reply to conversations explicitly assigned to them, ensuring they stay focused on their specific tasks. |

## **Managing Team Records**

### Editing Teams
Select the team, modify members or supervisors, and click **[Save]**. Changes are active for all subsequent campaign assignments.

To remove an obsolete division, select the team and click **[Delete]**, then confirm the action.

<Tips>
## **Workflow Best Practices**

- **Data Security**: Perform a **[Download Data]** backup monthly to ensure you have an offline copy of your commercial relationships.
- **Communication Continuity**: Set up your operating hours and automatic response template *before* launching your first major campaign.
- **Load Balancing**: Use **Teams** to divide high-traffic days between different agent groups to prevent burnout.
- **Audit Trails**: Regularly review the **Members** tab to de-provision access for former employees or temporary contractors.
- **Marketing Agility**: Integrate Bitly early on to ensure all your campaign links are professional and trackable.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Invalid Invitation**: If an invite link fails, regenerate it from the dashboard; links often have security expiration windows.
- **Permission Denied**: If you cannot modify roles or save profile changes, verify that your account is set to **Owner** or **Manager**.
- **Silent Automations**: If automatic responses aren't firing, double-check that both your **Operating Hours** are saved and the **Toggle** switch in the Auto-Response menu is set to "Active."
- **Export Failures**: If the database download fails, check your internet stability; large backups require a consistent connection to finalize.
- **Token Generation Errors**: If the "Generate Token" button fails for Bitly, verify your Bitly password directly on their website first.
- **Desynchronized View**: Log out and log back in if you change a role and the dashboard doesn't immediately reflect new permissions.

</Kendala>
