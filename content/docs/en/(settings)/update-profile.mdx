Update Profile is a centralized settings menu in NoBox.AI used to manage both individual **User Profiles** and organizational **Company Details**.

This module allows users to maintain accurate personal data, adjust account preferences, and update official company information registered within the platform. By ensuring profile data remains current, users improve team coordination, enhance communication quality, and ensure administrative compliance.

## **User Profile Settings**

The **User Profile** section contains personal identification data used to recognize your account within the NoBox.AI ecosystem.

From this page, you can update your profile avatar, refresh contact information, toggle email notifications, adjust interface directions, and manage account security credentials.

### **User Profile Form Fields**

| Field           | Description                                                                                                                                                                          |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Profile Image   | Upload a clear headshot to act as your visual identifier within the system, helping teammates recognize your account during task assignments.                                        |
| First Name      | Your primary name, displayed across the platform and in various internal/external communications.                                                                                    |
| Last Name       | Your surname to complete your account identity.                                                                                                                                      |
| Position        | Your current job title or role within the company, used as internal reference for group assignments.                                                                                 |
| Email Address   | An active email address used for receiving system notifications, security alerts, and campaign reports.                                                                              |
| Phone           | Your direct contact number for internal team coordination.                                                                                                                           |
| Direction       | Configures the navigation bar orientation: <br/> - **LTR (Left To Right)**: Standard Western layout (default). <br/> - **RTL (Right To Left)**: For specific linguistic preferences. |
| Old Password    | Your current active password. Required only if you intend to change your credentials.                                                                                                |
| New Password    | The new secure password you wish to set. Use a strong combination of characters.                                                                                                     |
| Repeat Password | Confirms the new password to prevent entry errors.                                                                                                                                   |

### **How to Update Your User Profile**

1. Click the **User Icon** in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
2. Select the **[Profile]** menu to launch the configuration page.
3. Modify the desired personal or security information.
4. Click **[Update]** to finalize and commit the changes.

## **Company Details Settings**

**Company Details** contains the official corporate data associated with your organization's NoBox.AI tenant.

In this section, authorized users can update the business name, physical headquarters, official contact information, and global system language preferences.

### **Company Details Form Fields**

| Field             | Description                                                                                                                                                  |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Company           | The official name of the business entity registered in NoBox.AI.                                                                                             |
| City              | The city where the business headquarters or primary office is located.                                                                                       |
| MyNoBox Tenant Id | A critical unique identifier used to bridge the NoBox.AI CRM with the subscription portal. **Keep this ID secure** as it is required for feature activation. |
| Address           | Full physical address for official documentation and administrative audit purposes.                                                                          |
| Phone             | The official company phone number accessible to customers or business partners.                                                                              |
| Zip Code          | The postal code for shipping and regional indexing.                                                                                                          |
| Website           | Your official business URL for brand credibility.                                                                                                            |
| State/Province    | The administrative region or state of operation.                                                                                                             |
| Country           | The sovereign nation where the business is legally registered.                                                                                               |
| Default Language  | The primary language used by the NoBox.AI interface for your team.                                                                                           |

### **How to Update Company Details**

1. Click the **User Icon** in the top-right corner.
2. Select **[Company Details]** from the menu.
3. Edit the organizational fields that require updating.
4. Click **[Update]** to apply the changes across the entire tenant.

<Tips>
## **Operational Tips**

- **Visual Recognition**: Use a professional profile photo to help colleagues identify your contributions in the shared inbox more quickly.
- **Active Monitoring**: Ensure your email is current to receive critical "Campaign Failure" or "Security Alert" notifications immediately.
- **Security Protocols**: Regularly update your password using the User Profile section to adhere to corporate security best practices.
- **Tenant Integrity**: Never share your **Tenant Id** publicly; it is for internal administrative use and support verification only.
- **Localization**: If your team is primarily based in a specific region, set the **Default Language** immediately to improve onboarding speed.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

- **Update Failure**: If the "Update" button does not save, check for red validation errors in mandatory fields (e.g., improperly formatted email or missing phone number).
- **Upload Errors**: If your profile photo fails to upload, ensure the file is under 2MB and in standard JPG or PNG format.
- **Forgotten Tenant Id**: If you lose your Tenant Id, check your initial registration confirmation email or contact your Master Admin.
- **Password Mismatch**: If you get a "Password mismatch" error, re-type the new password in both the "New" and "Repeat" fields carefully.
- **Session Latency**: If profile changes don't appear on the dashboard after saving, log out and log back in to force a metadata refresh.
- **Display Glitches**: Clear your browser cache if the profile forms appear misaligned or contain overlapping text.

</Kendala>
