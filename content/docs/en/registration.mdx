## **Registering a New User and New Business**

This section is for users who do not yet have a business on NoBox.AI and want to create an account along with a new business profile.

By following these steps, you can create a personal account and immediately begin managing your own business through the NoBox.AI platform.

### Preparation Before Registration

Before starting the registration process, ensure you have:

*   An active email address that you can access.
*   A valid phone number.
*   Basic information about the business you intend to create.
*   A stable internet connection.

### Steps to Register a New Business

Follow these steps to register on NoBox.AI with a new business:

1.  Open your browser and visit [id.NoBox.AI](https://id.NoBox.AI/).
2.  Click the option **"[Don't have an account? Register]"**.
3.  Complete the registration form with your requested personal information.
4.  Select the **"Create a new business"** option if you wish to set up a new business account.
5.  Enter detailed information about your business.
6.  Click the **"[Register]"** button to submit your registration form.
7.  Check your email inbox and click the verification link. Check your spam folder if the email is not found in your main inbox.

### After Successful Registration

Once your account has been verified, you can:

*   Log in to the system using your email and password.
*   Access the main NoBox.AI dashboard.
*   Manage your business data.
*   Configure your user profile.
*   Add team members.

If you wish to join an existing business instead, follow the steps below.

## **Registration and Joining an Existing Business**

This section is for users who have received an invitation from a business and wish to join as a member.

With this method, you do not need to create a new business; you will be directly connected to a business that is already running.

### Preparation Before Joining

Before registering, ensure that you have:

*   Received an invitation code from the business owner.
*   Confirmed that the invitation code is still active.
*   Prepared an email address that has not yet been registered on NoBox.AI.

### Steps to Join a Business

Follow these steps to register and join an existing business:

1.  Open your browser and visit the [login page](https://id.NoBox.AI/Account/Login/?ReturnUrl=%2F).
2.  Click the **[Don't have an account? Register]** button to start the process.
3.  Fill out the registration form with accurate data (name, email, password).
4.  Select the option **"Join an existing Business"**.
5.  Enter the valid invitation code provided by the business owner.
6.  Click the **[Register]** button to submit your registration data.
7.  Check your email and click the verification link to activate your account.

### After Successfully Joining

Once your account is verified and you have successfully joined:

*   Access business data according to your assigned permissions.
*   Use features according to your specific role.
*   Collaborate with the team.
*   View business activities.
*   Manage assigned tasks.

If all the steps above are followed correctly, you should be able to join an existing business on the NoBox.AI platform. If you encounter issues or do not receive a verification email, check your spam folder or contact NoBox.AI customer support for further assistance.

## **Usage Tips**

If you encounter issues during registration, consider the following:

**Account Checks**
- Ensure the email has never been registered on NoBox.AI before.
- Double-check the data entered for accuracy.
- Ensure the email address is capable of receiving verification messages.

**System Checks**
- Ensure your internet connection is stable.
- Use the latest version of your browser.
- Refresh the page if an error occurs.
- Close and reopen the browser if necessary.
- Restart your computer if the system becomes unresponsive.

**Additional Solutions**
- Attempt to re-register after a few minutes.
- Check the spam or promotions folder in your email.
- Ensure the NoBox.AI server is operating normally.
- Temporarily disable firewalls or VPNs if they are obstructing access.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter obstacles during the registration process, follow these steps:

- Check your spam or promotions folder if the verification email does not arrive.
- Ensure the invitation code (invite code) you entered is correct and still active.
- Use an email address that has never been registered on NoBox.AI before.
- Maintain a stable internet connection while filling out the form.
- Refresh the registration page if technical issues or system errors occur.
- Contact the support team if registration still fails or the verification link does not work.

</Kendala>
