AI Agents in NoBox.AI are intelligent virtual assistants that serve as the front line for automating your digital interactions. These agents are capable of answering customer questions, collecting lead data, and operating 24/7 without interruption across various communication platforms.

The goal is to increase customer service efficiency while ensuring every complex conversation can be intelligently handed off to a **human agent** when necessary.

### **AI Agent Dialog Fields**

| Field | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Identity name for your AI Agent. |
| **Model** | The AI Engine used. |
| **Behavior** | Description of the AI's personality and primary tasks in answering messages. |
| **Category** | Grouping agents based on business function. |
| **Note** | Internal notes for admin reference. |
| **Welcome Message** | Automated greeting when starting a conversation with a customer. |
| **Agent Transfer Conditions** | Conditions or keywords that trigger the AI to hand off the chat to a human. |

After saving, the initial status of the AI Agent will be **Draft**. Click the **Publish** button when you are ready to activate it.

## **Editing and Deleting AI Agent Data**

### **Edit AI Agent Data**
Select the record you want to change in the table, adjust the behavior or other settings, and then click **[Save]**.

### **Delete AI Agent Data**
Select the AI Agent to be deleted, click the **[Delete]** button, and confirm your action in the dialog that appears.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting AI Agent data is permanent. The deleted agent will immediately stop serving customers, and all its configuration data will be lost from the system.
</Callout>

## **Usage Tips**

Optimize your AI Agent's performance with these tips:

- Provide a detailed **Behavior** description so the AI understands the limits of information it can provide to customers.
- Use the **Welcome Message** to automatically ask for basic customer information (such as Name or Purpose).
- Define clear **Transfer Conditions** so customers still get solutions from a human when the AI is unable to answer.
- Conduct testing in **Draft** status before publishing the agent to a public platform.
- Use the latest AI models to get more natural and accurate response quality.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues with an AI Agent, please follow this guide:

- **AI Not Responding**: Ensure the agent's status is **Published** and your communication platform (WA/Telegram) is connected.
- **Irrelevant AI Answers**: Review the instructions in the **Behavior** column and provide more specific guidance.
- **AI Credits Exhausted**: Check your remaining AI quota in the Subscription menu and top up if necessary.
- **Failed to Transfer to Human**: Check if there are human agents who are online and ready to receive the chat handoff.
- **Changes Not Saved**: Ensure a stable internet connection when clicking the **Save** button or try reloading the page.

</Kendala>
