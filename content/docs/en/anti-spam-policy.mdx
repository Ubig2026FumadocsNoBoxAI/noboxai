This policy applies to all NoBox Services used for sending commercial emails or bulk messages. We have a zero-tolerance commitment towards spamming activities to maintain system quality and the sending reputation for all users.

You agree to this Anti-Spam Policy as an absolute prerequisite for using NoBox.AI services.

---

## **Definition of Sending Permission**

NoBox only allows message delivery to people who have given explicit permission (*Permission*) to you. This permission is considered valid if obtained through:

- Submission of email address/contact when downloading assets from your website or ordering products.
- Conscious newsletter registration through a form on the website.
- Participation in a contest, event, or survey where participants have been informed they will receive marketing emails.
- Filling out business cards at a trade show booth stating willingness to receive commercial information.

<Callout type="error" title="Primary Rule">
**"Do not use NoBox services to send messages to people without provable explicit permission."** Violation of this rule may lead to permanent termination of your account.
</Callout>

---

## **100% Compliance Practice Rules**

To ensure your account remains secure and compliant with the rules, you are required to perform the following steps:

1.  **Permission-Based Contacts Only**: Never use contact databases that you purchased, borrowed, rented, or obtained from any third party.
2.  **Accurate Identity**: Ensure "From" information, subject lines, and message headers reflect the actual identity of the sender and are not misleading.
3.  **Unsubscribe Option**: Include a clear Unsubscribe link or opt-out instructions in every promotional message.
4.  **Physical Address**: You are required to include a valid physical postal address in every marketing message sent.
5.  **Ad Identification**: Clearly state that the message is a form of advertising or commercial solicitation.

---

## **Services and Monitoring**

We reserve the right at any time to request proof of written permission for databases you upload to the NoBox.AI system. We periodically monitor spam complaint levels (*Spam Reports*) to detect any suspicious activity.

---

<Tips>
## **Tips to Avoid Spam Filters**

- **Double Opt-In**: Use a two-step verification system when customers register to ensure their emails are correct and valid.
- **Quality Content**: Avoid overly bombastic words or "Clickbait" subjects that annoy message recipients.
- **List Cleaning**: Regularly remove contacts that never respond or frequently result in delivery failures (*Bounce*).
- **Honor Unsubscribe**: Immediately process unsubscribe requests (maximum within 10 days per policy).

</Tips>
