This integration ensures your shop's chat performance remains high, increases buyer trust, and optimizes sales conversions without needing to be standby in front of the screen at all times.

## **Connecting a Shopee Account**

You need to link your Shopee seller account to the NoBox Desktop system for message synchronization.

### **Account Addition Steps:**

1. Click the **[Account]** module ➔ **[Add Account]** button.
2. Select the **Shopee** channel and complete the account name and category details.
3. Click **[Save]**.

## **Configuration and Synchronization (Save Session)**

After the account is registered, you need to authenticate so that NoBox can access chat features.

### **How to Activate Session:**
- Open the NoBox Desktop interface (Chat Session).
- Log in to your Shopee account through the available internal browser.
- Once successfully logged in, click the **[Save Session]** button.
- Ensure the confirmation "Save session successfully" appears in the logs tab. Your Shopee account is now ready for use in NoBox.AI.

## **Messaging and Promotions Management**

### **Centralized Inbox**
All chats entering your Shopee Seller Centre will be pulled into the **[Messages]** ➔ **[Inbox]** menu. You can reply to customer chats with templates or quick replies to maintain shop chat performance metrics.

### **Seller Promotional Campaigns**
Use your Shopee account to send broadcast/promotional messages to specific target customers. Monitor sending progress through the **Send Messages** tab to ensure the campaign runs smoothly.

## **Editing and Deleting Account Data**

### **Edit Account Data**
Navigate to the **[Account]** menu on the NoBox.AI website, select the relevant Shopee account, adjust info details, and then click **[Save]**.

### **Delete Account Data**
Select the account you want to permanently disconnect and click the **[Delete]** button.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting an account will revoke all Shopee session authentication on the NoBox.AI system. You will have to log in again and save the session if you want to use the account in the future.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Optimize your Shopee shop management with these tips:

- Always save the session (**Save Session**) after logging in so that message synchronization runs in the background automatically.
- Use polite and informative language typical of marketplaces to maintain your shop rating.
- Utilize the **Inbox** feature to reply to chats from various Shopee shops (if you have multiple shops) on a single screen.
- Ensure the internet connection on the NoBox Desktop server computer is stable so that automated chatbot responses are not hampered.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues in the Shopee Automation service, please follow these steps:

- **Chats Not Entering NoBox**: Check if the session status in NoBox Desktop is still active or requires re-login (Expired Session).
- **Fail to Login**: Ensure email/username and password are correct, or check if there's a captcha/OTP verification from Shopee.
- **Broadcast Messages Delay**: Check the queue on the Send Messages tab or ensure Shopee daily sending limits are not reached.
- **Messy Chat Display**: Refresh the NoBox.AI browser page or clear your browser cache.
- **Shopee Notifications Appear on Phone but Not NoBox**: Perform a manual sync by clicking the account refresh button on NoBox Desktop.

</Kendala>
