NoBox.AI uses cookie technology to improve your navigation experience on our platform. Cookies help us understand how you interact with our services, remember your preferences, and maintain your login session security without compromising your personal data privacy.

Our cookie usage is completely safe and is not used to run harmful programs on your device.

---

## **What are Cookies?**

Cookies are very small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Their function is as a short-term reminder for the browser to recognize your session identity, such as language choices or login status, so you don't have to enter the same information repeatedly.

---

## **Types of Cookies Used**

NoBox.AI implements two main cookie status types:
- **Session Cookies**: Automatically deleted when you close the browser tab.
- **Persistent Cookies**: Stored on your device until they expire or you delete them manually.

---

## **Third-Party Cookies**

We have a strict policy not to allow third-party tracking companies to place cookies on our site for advertising purposes. However, when we embed content such as videos (for example from YouTube), those platforms may place technical cookies for bandwidth management or media players.

NoBox always strives to use "Privacy Mode" when embedding external content to minimize third-party tracking of your data.

---

## **Manual Cookie Management**

Almost all modern browsers provide full control for you to manage or block cookies through the settings menu.
