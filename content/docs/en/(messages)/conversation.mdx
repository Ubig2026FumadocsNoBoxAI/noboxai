You can filter conversation data based on **Status**, **Channel**, **Account**, and other available filters.  
To view conversation details, click <img src="/images/en/Go%20to%20message.png" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" alt="Open Message" /> **[Open Conversation]**, and you will be redirected to the selected conversation.
