## **Data Filtering in the Conversations Menu**

In the Conversations menu, users can filter communication data based on the following categories:

### 1. Status Filter

Used to filter conversation data based on its handling status, such as active, in-progress, or completed.

### 2. Channel Filter

Used to display conversation data based on the communication channel used, such as WhatsApp, Telegram, or other platforms.

### 3. Account Filter

Used to filter conversations based on the specific account or number connected within the system.

### 4. Additional Filters

In addition to the main filters, other filters are available for specific data monitoring needs.

Users can combine multiple filters simultaneously to obtain more specific and accurate data results.

---

## **Viewing Conversation Details**

To view detailed individual conversation data, follow these steps:

1. Select one of the conversation entries on the list.
2. Click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/Go%20to%20message.png" alt="Open Message" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] **[Open Conversation]**.
3. The system will direct you to the selected conversation page.

In the conversation detail view, users can see the message content, contact information, and the full communication history.
