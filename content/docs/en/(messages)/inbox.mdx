It streamlines the customer journey from initial contact to resolution, ensuring that your marketing and support efforts are organized, responsive, and highly effective.

## **Filtering, Archiving, and Searching Conversations**

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
- **Conversation Archive** is a feature for storing or sorting messages that may no longer be relevant or require immediate response. By archiving, users can clean up their main conversation list without permanently deleting the messages. Click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/Arsip.png" alt="Archive" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] to display archived conversations.
- You can **filter conversations** based on Status, Channel, Account, Contact, etc. To filter, click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/filter.png" width="500" /> ] and the filter options will appear. Select the desired data and click the **[Show]** button. Click **[Reset]** to clear the conversation filters.
</Callout>

## **How to Enable Auto Complete or Auto Archive**

To enable the Auto Complete and Auto Archive features, follow these steps:

1. Open the **[Settings]** module ➔ click the **[Business Profile]** menu.
2. Activate the category for **Auto Complete** or **Auto Archive**.
3. Specify after how many hours the conversation will be automatically closed or archived.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Arsip%20Otomatis.png"
  alt="Auto Archive Settings"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

### **Auto Complete**

On the Inbox page, there is an auto-complete feature used to configure whether conversations are automatically marked as completed. This feature applies to chats with "In Progress" status.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Selesai%20Otomatis.png"
  alt="Auto Complete Settings"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

If you want to mark a conversation as completed manually without waiting for the auto-timer, click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/titik%20tiga.png" alt="Menu" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] and select **[Mark as Done]** from the pop-up.

### Auto Archive

On the Inbox page, there is an auto-archive feature used to configure whether conversations are automatically archived. This feature applies to chats with "Completed" status.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Kotak%20Masuk.png"
  alt="Inbox View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

If you want to archive immediately without waiting for auto-archive, click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/Arsip.png" alt="Archive" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] to archive the conversation instantly.

## **Message Management**

### Replying to Messages

You can reply to incoming messages directly through NoBox. Open a conversation and respond to the customer's inquiries.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Balas%20Percakapan.png"
  alt="How to Reply to Messages"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto"
/>

Message delivery status is indicated by icons. If a message is marked with [ <img src="/images/id/Percakapan%20terkirim.png" alt="Sent" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ], it has been sent; if it is marked with [ <img src="/images/id/terkirim.png" alt="Not Sent" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ], it has not yet been sent.

### Deleting Messages

The Inbox page includes a delete feature to remove messages from a conversation; this feature only deletes the message within NoBox. To delete a message, select one and click **[Delete]**; a confirmation prompt will appear. Click **[Yes]** to confirm.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting a message only removes it from NoBox; it does not delete it from the original platform (e.g., WhatsApp). Deletions are permanent and cannot be undone.
</Callout>

### Sending Files

The Inbox page allows sending various types of files, including images, photos, voice notes, PDFs, Excel files, etc. To send a file, click the icon [ <img src="/images/id/kirim%20file.png" alt="Send File" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] and select the file type to send [ <img src="/images/id/tipe%20kirim%20file.png" alt="File Type" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ]. A **[Select File]** dialog will appear for you to upload and send.

<ImageZoom

### Quick Replies

NoBox includes a Quick Reply feature for message templates, allowing you to respond faster using text or media (images, GIFs, Videos). To send a quick reply:

1. In the message input field, type **"/"**.
2. A list of available quick replies will appear. Select one.
3. The content will automatically populate the message field; press the [ <img src="/images/id/send.png" alt="Send" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] icon to send.

## **Usage Tips**

Here are some tips to maximize your use of the Inbox menu:

- Check for new messages regularly to ensure no customer is overlooked.
- Use filtering to simplify finding specific conversations.
- Utilize the pinning feature to keep track of important chats.
- Apply tags to categorize various types of conversations.
- Enable auto-archive to keep your view tidy.
- Add notes to facilitate coordination between agents.
- Use quick replies to speed up response times for customers.

</Tips>

## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues while using the Inbox menu, take these steps:

- Ensure your internet connection is stable.
- Refresh the page if conversations fail to appear or update.
- Verify the status of connected accounts and channels.
- Ensure agents have been correctly assigned.
- Check auto-complete and auto-archive settings.
- Log out and log back in if features are not functioning normally.
- Clear your browser cache if the display is incorrect.
- Contact the support team if problems persist.
