Funnel is a feature in NoBox.AI used to categorize stages of the marketing and customer support process. This feature is visualized as a workflow that helps your team monitor the customer journey, from initial interaction to service completion or transaction.

With a structured funnel system, you can evaluate the effectiveness of campaigns and the team's response speed at every customer touchpoint.

## **Funnel Data Management**

### Adding a New Funnel
1. Click the **[Funnel]** menu ➔ **[Add Funnel]** button.
2. Enter the name of the funnel stage (e.g., "New Prospect," "Negotiation," "Paid").
3. Click **[Save]** to register the stage into the system.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Tambah%20Funnel.png"
  alt="Add Funnel Stage"
  width={800}

### Editing and Deleting Funnel Data
- **Edit**: Select the funnel data, adjust its naming, and click **[Save]**. Changes will be immediately reflected across all connected customer profiles.
- **Delete**: Select the data you wish to remove and click the **[Delete]** button.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
Deleting funnel data is permanent. Contacts in a deleted stage will lose their original stage labels. Ensure you have moved contacts to another stage before deleting funnel data.
</Callout>

## **How to Use the Funnel**

Funnels can be applied directly while you interact with customers on the conversation page.

1. Open the **[Inbox]** menu and select a chat.
2. Click the information icon [ <img src="/images/id/information%20icon.png" alt="Information" className="inline h-5 w-auto mx-1" /> ] in the top-right corner to open the detail panel.
3. In the **Funnel** section, click the *dropdown* and select the appropriate stage for that customer.

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

Optimize your business flow with these funnel management tips:

- Create logical funnel stages that follow your actual customer journey.
- Use short and clear naming (e.g., "MQL," "SQL," "Closed Won").
- Perform regular evaluations; if too many contacts pile up at one stage, it indicates a bottleneck in your business process.
- Utilize the funnel to prioritize chat handling; focus on customers at the final stages (approaching a transaction).
- Train your team to be disciplined in updating funnel status whenever progress occurs in a conversation.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter issues with the Funnel feature, follow these steps:

- **Funnel Does Not Appear in Chat Dropdown**: Ensure you have pressed the **Save** button when creating the funnel and that its status is active.
- **Failed to Change Funnel Status**: Check your internet connection or try refreshing the dashboard page.
- **Funnel Data Deleted Accidentally**: Unfortunately, deleted data is permanent. Immediately recreate the stage and re-associate it with the relevant contacts.
- **Restricted Access Rights**: Ensure your account role has the authority to add or edit CRM data.
- **Application Slows Down When Selecting Funnel**: Clear your browser cache to speed up dropdown data loading.

</Kendala>
