NoBox provides Software as a Service (SaaS) products to millions of people to solve their business problems. Security is a core component of our offering, and is reflected in our people, processes, and products. This page covers topics such as data security, operational security, and physical security to explain how we offer security to our customers.

Strong security integration ensures that every piece of data you manage at NoBox.AI is protected with the highest industry standards, providing peace of mind for your business growth.

---

## **Organizational Security**

We have an Information Security Management System that takes into account our security objectives and the associated risks and mitigations for all interested parties. We implement strict policies and procedures covering the security, availability, processing, integrity, and confidentiality of customer data.

---

## **Employee Background Checks**

Every employee undergoes a background verification process. We do this to verify their criminal records, previous employment records if any, and educational background. Until these checks are conducted, employees are not assigned tasks that could pose a risk to users.

---

## **Security Awareness**

Every employee, upon induction, signs a confidentiality agreement and acceptable use policy, after which they undergo training in information security, privacy, and compliance.

### Ongoing Evaluation:
- **Internal Training**: We evaluate employee understanding through tests and quizzes to determine which topics require further training.
- **Community Education**: We educate employees continuously about information security, privacy, and compliance within our internal communities.
- **Privacy Innovation**: We host internal events to raise awareness and encourage innovation in security and privacy.

---

## **Dedicated Security and Privacy Teams**

We have dedicated security and privacy teams that proactively implement and manage our security programs.

- **System Defense**: Designing and maintaining our defense systems.
- **Network Monitoring**: Constantly monitoring the network to detect suspicious activity.
- **Internal Consultation**: Providing domain-specific guidance to our engineering teams to ensure secure code implementation.

## **User Security Tips**

In addition to protection on our end, you can enhance your account security by:

- **Strong Passwords**: Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols for your NoBox account password.
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable additional security features if available on connected email platforms.
- **Beware of Phishing**: NoBox never asks for your password through private chat or email.
- **Regular Logout**: Always log out of browser sessions if using public devices.

</Tips>

## **Reporting Security Issues**

If you find or suspect a security vulnerability in our system:

- **Contact the Security Team**: Immediately send a report via email to [security@NoBox.AI](mailto:security@NoBox.AI).
- **Report Details**: Provide a full description of your findings so our team can perform an audit immediately.
- **Follow-up**: Our team will provide a response within 24-48 business hours regarding the status of the security investigation.

</Kendala>
