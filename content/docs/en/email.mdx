**Email Automation** in NoBox.AI allows you to send marketing messages, product information, or service updates automatically and in bulk. Through integration of the SMTP (*Simple Mail Transfer Protocol*), you can connect various popular email provider services (Gmail, Outlook, or custom domains) to reach customer inboxes timely.

This feature is ideal for building long-term relationships through Newsletters, automated invoice notifications, and new product introduction campaigns with high delivery precision.

---

## **Connecting an Email Account**

To start sending, you must register a sender email account into the NoBox.AI infrastructure.

### **Account Addition Steps:**

1. Go to the **[Account]** module ➔ click the **[Add Account]** button.
2. Select the **Email** channel from the available options.
3. Complete the account identity data (Account Name and Category).
4. Click **[Save]**.

## **SMTP Configuration**

After basic data is saved, you need to enter technical sending details so that NoBox can send emails on your behalf.

### **Configuration Stages:**
- Select an email provider (If the list is not available, use the Custom/SMTP option).
- Enter the appropriate **SMTP Host** and **Port** (e.g., `smtp.gmail.com` port `587` for Gmail).
- Enter the **Email Address** and **Password / App Password**.
- Click **[OK]** or **[Save]**. The system will perform an automatic connection test.

## **Email Campaign Management**

### **Sending Promotions**
Just like other channels, you can create email campaigns through the **[Promotions]** module. Select the Email channel and specify the sender account you have configured. You can include file attachments and images directly into the email body.

### **Delivery Monitoring**
Monitor the status of each sent email (Queue, Processed, Sent, or Failed) through the monitoring dashboard. You can also see email replies (if supported) through the **Inbox** menu for centralized conversation synchronization.

---

## **Editing and Deleting Account Data**

### **Data Changes While Account is Active**
If you need to change your email password or edit SMTP details, navigate to the **[Account]** menu on the NoBox.AI website, select the relevant account, make changes, and press **[Save]**.

### **Account Deletion**
Select the account that is no longer used and click the **[Delete]** button.

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
The system will permanently delete all stored SMTP credentials. Ensure there are no scheduled email campaigns relying on that account before performing a deletion.
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Email Automation Usage Tips**

- **Use App Password**: For services like Gmail, do not use your primary account password. Create an *App Password* in your Google account security settings for a more secure third-party connection.
- **Rate Limit**: Do not send too many emails in a short time to avoid being detected as spam by the destination provider and to maintain your IP/Domain reputation.
- **Engaging Subject**: Use informative email subjects to increase the *Open Rate* from your customers.
- **Content Verification**: Always send trial emails to a personal address before broadcasting to thousands in your database.

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If email delivery is hampered, please follow these steps:

- **Authentication Error**: Re-check if the Email and Password/App Password are correct. Often problems arise from passwords that are edited but not updated in the system.
- **Email Enters Spam Folder**: Ensure your email content does not contain words that trigger excessive spam filters and add SPF/DKIM records to your business email domain.
- **SMTP Connection Timeout**: Ensure the Port you are using is opened by your internet connection provider (e.g., Port 587 or 465).
- **Process Paused**: Ensure account status on the dashboard remains "Active". If consecutive failures occur, the system might suspend delivery to protect your account reputation.
- **Attachment Too Large**: Ensure the total size of attached files does not exceed limits (usually max 10MB-20MB) to avoid rejection by the recipient server.

</Kendala>
