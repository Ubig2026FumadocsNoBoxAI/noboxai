This process requires access to the email address you previously registered to ensure that the requester is the legitimate account owner.

## **Password Reset Procedure**

Here are the practical stages to get a new password:

### 1. Reset Request
On the [id.nobox.ai](https://nobox.ai/) website login page, click the **[Forgot Password]** link. Enter your registered email address and complete the *reCAPTCHA* verification, then click the **[Reset My Password]** button.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Kirim%20ke%20email.png"
  alt="Password Reset Request Facility"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

### 2. Email Verification
The system will send an email containing a unique recovery link. Open your email inbox, look for a message from NoBox.AI, and click the button or link provided. You will be directly directed to the new password creation page.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Email.png"
  alt="Example Account Recovery Email"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

### 3. Creating a New Password
Enter a new strong password and repeat it in the confirmation field. Avoid using easily guessable passwords. Once done, click the **[Reset My Password]** button.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Reset.png"
  alt="New Password Setting Form"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

If a success message appears, please click **[OK]** and you will be directed back to the main login page to enter using your new credentials.

<Tips>
## **Password Security Tips**

- Use a combination of at least 8 characters consisting of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols (e.g., !@#$).
- Do not use the same password for your NoBox.AI account as your personal email password for double security.
- Update your password periodically (every 3-6 months) to prevent unauthorized access.
- Use the save password feature in a trusted browser for easy access in the future.

</Tips>

## **Handling Reset Issues**

If you encounter obstacles during the recovery process, please check the following solutions:

- **Reset Email Not Received**: Check the **Spam** or **Promotions** folder in your email. If it hasn't arrived within 5 minutes, please try resending the request.
- **Reset Link Expired**: Reset links are usually only valid for 1 hour for security. If it's no longer working, please make a new request.
- **Reset Link Error**: Use another browser or open the link in *Incognito* mode to avoid issues with old browser cache data.
- **Forgot Registered Email Address**: Please contact our support team through official communication channels by attaching proof of ownership or the last transaction.

</Kendala>
